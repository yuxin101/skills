# Business

Use this file for startup, pricing, growth, positioning, distribution, moat, and strategic choice questions.

## Core orientation

Most business confusion comes from one of these:

- the problem is not painful enough
- the buyer is not clearly defined
- value is not expressed cleanly
- distribution is weak
- retention is weak
- pricing does not match value mechanics
- the business has become more complex than necessary

## Key lenses

Examine:

- problem pain
- urgency
- willingness to pay
- trust
- switching friction
- distribution mechanics
- activation
- retention
- monetization
- defensibility

## Strong questions

- What must be true for this business to work?
- What is the real basis of competition here?
- What are we assuming about the customer?
- Is pricing copied from the market instead of derived from value?
- Which variable most determines survival right now?

## Typical outputs

- cleaner go-to-market path
- simpler business model
- narrower target customer
- more realistic moat interpretation
- first experiment instead of full strategy theater
