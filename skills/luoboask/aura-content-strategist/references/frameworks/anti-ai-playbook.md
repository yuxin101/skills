# Anti-AI Detection Playbook

2026 platforms have AI content detection. Goal: **credible human feel**, not perfection.

## Four Dimensions

### 1. Copy (文案层)

**Add human texture**:
- Colloquial filler: "救命" "绝了" "姐妹们" "真的" "OMG"
- Slight grammar imperfections: "这个也太好用了吧" (redundant 也)
- Personal anecdotes: "我家猫差点把这个打翻" "用了三天发现..."
- Mid-sentence corrections: "这个是...不对应该说是..."

**Avoid AI tells**:
- ❌ Perfect grammar, no filler words
- ❌ Corporate tone: "本产品具有..." "值得推荐"
- ❌ Overly structured: "首先...其次...最后..."
- ❌ Generic praise: "非常好用" "强烈推荐"

### 2. Visual (视觉层)

**Add environmental realism**:
- Background clutter: coffee cup, phone, keys in frame
- Imperfect lighting: natural window light with shadows
- Handheld shake: slight blur, not tripod-perfect
- Non-centered composition: rule of thirds, not dead center
- Real-world context: desk, bed, kitchen counter

**Avoid AI tells**:
- ❌ Perfect symmetry, studio lighting
- ❌ Stock photo vibe: too clean, too staged
- ❌ Floating objects on pure white background
- ❌ Unrealistic perfection: no dust, no shadows, no texture

### 3. Behavior (行为层)

**Mimic human posting patterns**:
- Randomized post times: 19:23, not 19:00 sharp
- Post-then-edit: publish → wait 2-5 min → edit typo
- Irregular intervals: not every day at same time
- Occasional breaks: skip 1-2 days randomly

**Avoid bot patterns**:
- ❌ Fixed schedule: every day 9:00 AM
- ❌ Instant multi-platform cross-post
- ❌ Perfect consistency: same format every time
- ❌ No edits, no typos, no corrections

### 4. Comments (评论层)

**First self-comment strategy**:
- Post within 1-3 minutes of publishing
- Add "forgot to mention" details
- Use casual tone: "对了忘说了，这个..."
- Simulate typo correction: "刚发现打错字了哈哈"

**Reply templates** (vary each time):
- ✅ "哈哈哈对！我也是这样" (enthusiastic)
- ✅ "嗯嗯这个确实" (casual agreement)
- ✅ "啊我去试试！" (excited)
- ❌ "感谢支持！" (robotic)
- ❌ "谢谢你的评论" (corporate)

## Platform-Specific Tactics

### Xiaohongshu
- Use emoji断句, not perfect punctuation
- Add 语气词: "啦" "呀" "哦" "嘛"
- Occasional typo in body (not title): "在" → "再"
- Self-reply with "补充" or "更新"

### Pinterest
- Description: conversational English, not SEO-stuffed
- Vary sentence length: short punchy + longer explanatory
- Use contractions: "you'll" not "you will"
- Add personal voice: "I love..." "My go-to..."

## Checklist Before Publishing

- [ ] Copy has ≥2 colloquial filler words?
- [ ] Visual has environmental imperfection?
- [ ] Post time not on the hour?
- [ ] First self-comment drafted?
- [ ] Reply templates personalized?
- [ ] No sentence screams "AI wrote this"?
