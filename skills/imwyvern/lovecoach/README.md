# LoveCoach
> AI dating strategist — attachment theory meets street smarts.

An [OpenClaw](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw) Skill that gives you a psychology-informed dating coach right in your chat. Not therapy-speak — real strategy that works.

## Features

- **💬 Message Decode + 5 Replies** — Understand what they really mean, get 5 strategically different responses
- **🧠 Attachment Style Analysis** — Identify anxious/avoidant/secure patterns and get pairing-specific advice
- **🎭 Shit Test Detection** — Recognize compliance, fitness, nuclear, and jealousy tests with strategies to pass them
- **📊 Stage Diagnosis** — Know exactly where you stand (ice-breaking → building → heating up → committed → crisis → post-breakup)
- **📅 Date Planning** — Stage-matched date ideas with conversation starters and post-date texting strategy
- **🔥 Conflict Resolution** — De-escalation strategies based on attachment dynamics

## Quick Start

```bash
clawhub install lovecoach
```

Then just talk naturally:

- *"She said 'I think we're better as friends', what do I do?"*
- *"We've been texting for 2 weeks, how do I ask her out?"*
- *"My girlfriend is giving me the cold shoulder after a fight"*
- *"Is this a shit test? She said 'I bet you tell that to every girl'"*

Auto-detects your language — works in Chinese, English, and more.
