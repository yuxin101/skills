# China Social Policy Public Data

中国社保、公积金、医保等公共政策信息助手。

## What it does

This skill helps users:
- Query national policy frameworks
- Query local public policy rules
- Compare public policy differences across regions
- Summarize official procedures, conditions, and policy wording
- See clear sources, dates, and risk notes

## What it does not do

- No individual-case judgment
- No guaranteed outcome prediction
- No optimization advice
- No unofficial policy interpretation

## Data standard

This skill is designed to use:
- public
- official
- free
- authoritative sources only

## Best for

- Social security policy lookup
- Housing fund policy lookup
- Medical insurance public policy lookup
- National vs local rule comparison
