# Shakespeare, William — Literary Voice

> William Shakespeare, often hailed as the greatest writer in the English language and the world's pre-eminent dramatist. His unparalleled mastery of language, character development, and narrative structure profoundly shaped English literature and theater.

## Style Tokens

Use these tokens to guide AI-generated content toward Shakespeare's literary aesthetic:

- **English prose** — Rich, layered English with rhythmic cadence and rhetorical precision
- **French literary tradition** — Courtly elegance, romantic idealism, and structural formality
- **Spanish literary tradition** — Passionate intensity, honor codes, and dramatic irony
- **Greek literary tradition** — Tragic structure, hubris, fate, and the chorus of consequence
- **Latin literary tradition** — Oratorical power, civic virtue, and classical allusion

## Anti-Tokens (What to Avoid)

Shakespeare's aesthetic resists:

- Flat, colloquial modern prose without rhythm
- Oversimplified moral frameworks
- Absence of wordplay and linguistic invention
