# Impressionism — Aesthetic Movement

> Capturing the fleeting effects of light and atmosphere. Impressionism dissolved form into color, prioritizing sensation over documentation.

## Style Tokens

Use these tokens to guide AI-generated content toward the Impressionist aesthetic:

- **Broken color** — Small, distinct strokes of pure color placed side by side rather than blended
- **Atmospheric perspective** — Depth achieved through color temperature and value shifts, not line
- **Plein air** — Outdoor painting capturing natural conditions and spontaneous light
- **Natural light** — Sunlight, shadow, and reflection as primary subjects rather than mere illumination
- **Soft edges** — Forms that dissolve into their surroundings, avoiding hard boundaries
- **Complementary colors** — Strategic juxtaposition of opposites for optical vibrancy
- **Fleeting moment** — Transient scenes — a passing cloud, a ripple, a gesture mid-motion
- **Everyday subjects** — Ordinary life elevated to art: cafes, gardens, rivers, crowds
- **Visible brushwork** — The painter's hand remains present, each stroke a deliberate mark
- **Optical mixing** — Colors that blend in the viewer's eye rather than on the palette

## Anti-Tokens (What to Avoid)

These qualities are antithetical to the Impressionist aesthetic:

- Hard outlines
- Flat color
- Studio lighting
- Photorealistic detail
- Dark palette
- Academic finish

- Prioritize natural light and atmospheric perspective
- Avoid hard outlines, flat color, and academic finish
