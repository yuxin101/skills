# Musician Manager — AI Band Manager

Turn OpenClaw into a full-service AI band manager for independent musicians.

## Capabilities

### 1. Venue Booking & Outreach
- Research venues by city, genre, capacity
- Draft professional booking emails
- Track outreach status (sent, replied, confirmed, rejected)
- Calendar management for shows
- Follow-up reminders

### 2. Social Media Content
- Generate post ideas based on band activity
- Create captions with proper hashtags for music audience
- Schedule content calendar (release countdown, behind-the-scenes, etc.)
- Cross-platform posting via Postiz/Larry skill
- Analytics tracking and optimization

### 3. Release Planning
- Create release timeline (8 weeks out → release day → post-release)
- Pre-save campaign management
- Playlist pitching email templates
- Social media blitz scheduling
- Fan email/SMS announcements

### 4. AI Music Video Production
- Full pipeline using Kling AI (see ai-music-video skill)
- Lyric transcription → scene concepts → image generation → video stitching
- Proven: NDGO "VIBE" music video — 20 scenes, 3 minutes, made overnight

### 5. Setlist Management
- Generate setlists based on venue type, set length, crowd energy flow
- Track which songs get best crowd response
- Suggest opening/closing songs based on data

### 6. Tour Logistics
- Route optimization between cities
- Venue load-in/soundcheck time tracking
- Day sheet generation
- Budget tracking (gas, hotels, food, merch)

### 7. Contract Analysis
- Review performance contracts for red flags
- Identify unfavorable terms (pay-to-play, excessive sound buy requirements)
- Suggest counter-offers
- Track payment terms and follow up on outstanding payments

### 8. Fan Engagement
- Email list management and newsletters
- DM response drafting
- Fan data analysis (where are they, what do they engage with)
- Street team coordination

## Voice Commands (Hey Boris)
When the voice assistant is active, these are natural language triggers:
- "Hey Boris, find me venues in [city] that book [genre] bands"
- "Hey Boris, draft a booking email for [venue]"
- "Hey Boris, what shows do we have this month?"
- "Hey Boris, post our new single announcement"
- "Hey Boris, create a release plan for [song name]"
- "Hey Boris, what's our setlist for tonight?"
- "Hey Boris, analyze this contract" (then share document)

## Integration Points
- **Telegram/WhatsApp:** Primary communication channel
- **Hey Boris:** Hands-free voice commands
- **Postiz:** Social media posting and analytics
- **ElevenLabs:** Voice responses and AI phone calls
- **Kling AI:** Music video generation
- **Google Calendar:** Show scheduling
- **Email:** Venue outreach, fan newsletters

## Target Audience
- Independent musicians (solo or band)
- Small to mid-level artists (0-100k monthly listeners)
- DIY artists who can't afford a manager
- Small labels managing multiple artists
- Booking agents looking for efficiency tools
