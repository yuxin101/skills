# Ad Copy AI System (from AudienceLab)

## Overview
A multi-step system that creates deeply resonant ad copy by first building detailed customer avatars, then channeling their emotions into ad scripts. Used by YourName at AudienceLab — proven framework.

## Pre-Step: Market Research
Ask: "Why would [niche] want [specific service]?"
This info feeds all future prompts.

## Step 1: Perfect Persona Prompting

Create 5 customer avatars, one for each Stage of Awareness (from Breakthrough Advertising):
1. **Unaware** — Doesn't know they have a problem
2. **Problem Aware** — Knows the problem, doesn't know solutions exist
3. **Solution Aware** — Knows solutions exist, doesn't know your product
4. **Product Aware** — Knows your product, hasn't bought yet
5. **Most Aware** — Knows your product, just needs the right offer

**Start with the problem-aware avatar.**

## Step 2: Diary Entry Method

Imagine you ARE the problem-aware avatar. Write a 1000+ word diary entry using:
- Passionate, visceral, emotional language
- Deepest feelings, fears, and desires
- Start with "Dear Diary,"
- Channel the psychographics, pain profile, desire profile, wants, and 3D profile

## Step 3: Ad Architecture

Rewrite the diary entry as an expert copywriter would — describing the same feelings/emotions with granular detail to the prospect. Use this 4-part structure:

### 1. INTERRUPT (5-30 seconds)
Grab attention using one of six hook types:
- **A: Towards Pleasure** — Paint the dream outcome
- **B: Away From Pain** — Highlight what they're suffering from
- **C: Shocking Statistic** — True stat related to their problem/desire
- **D: Physical Focus** — Point to something on screen/in hand ("See this document...")
- **E: High-Value Curiosity** — Lead with value but create curiosity gap
- **F: Testimonial Hook** — "Meet X who achieved Y..."

### 2. ENGAGE (15-30 seconds)
Showcase authority:
- Introduce yourself + credibility
- Why they should listen to YOU
- Example: "Hi my name's [Name] and since [year] I've helped over [number] people [achieve result] using [unique method]..."

### 3. EDUCATE (30 sec - 2 min)
Teach something valuable related to their pain/desire:
- Give 3 problems they face + solutions
- Share an insight they didn't know
- Build trust through value

### 4. OFFER (30 sec - 1 min)
Transition to the CTA:
- Clear next step ("Click the link below to...")
- Optional: Show what happens when they click
- Reduce friction by walking through the process

### Copy Rules:
1. Active Voice
2. Conversational, friendly but persuasive tone
3. Speak directly to reader
4. Perfect US grammar
5. 6th/7th-grade readability
6. Benefits split into: functional, dimensional, emotional

## Extra Layering Prompts (Style Variations)

After creating the base ad, create variations in these styles:
- **Harmon Brothers** — Humorous, entertaining, viral-style
- **Gary Halbert** — Direct response legend, urgent, personal
- **Dan Kennedy** — No BS, hard-hitting, deadline-driven
- **Batman** — Dark, dramatic, mysterious
- **Frank Kern** — Casual, story-based, lifestyle-focused

Each variation: 500 words, targeting problem-aware avatar. Don't mention the copywriter by name.

## Headlines & Hooks Generator

Create 25 unique headlines/hooks following these principles:
- Human connection — overlooked but engaging details
- Human interest — unusual, provocative, compelling backstory
- Relatable and compelling — "You've Got To Be Kidding Me!" reaction
- Almost unbelievable — intriguing but credible
- Legitimate payoff — delivers on the intrigue

### Example Headlines (gold standard):
- "Amazing Secret Discovered By One-Legged Golfer Adds 50 Yards To Your Drives..."
- "Tax-Slashing Secrets Of A College Drop-Out Who Scares The IRS To Death!"
- "How Does An Out-Of-Shape 55-Year-Old Golfer... Still Consistently Humiliate PGA Pros?"
- "Ex-Financial Advisor Breaks Silence On The 60-Second Investment Strategy..."
