## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Your product + target customer + launch date
        ↓
STEP 1 — Launch Intelligence Gathering
  └─ Product Hunt: top 50 launches in your category last 12 months
  └─ Extract: upvote patterns, maker comments, what drove traffic
  └─ Reddit: successful Show HN posts in your niche
  └─ Twitter: viral launch threads — format + engagement analysis
        ↓
STEP 2 — Competitor Launch Analysis
  └─ How did your top 3 competitors launch?
  └─ What worked? (still getting organic traffic from launch post)
  └─ What failed? (no engagement, no press, no traction)
  └─ What positioning did they use? (yours must be different)
        ↓
STEP 3 — Positioning Sharpening
  └─ Draft 5 one-liner value propositions
  └─ Test against competitor messaging (must be clearly different)
  └─ Score each by: clarity + specificity + desire + differentiation
  └─ Select winning positioning + headline
        ↓
STEP 4 — 30-Day Pre-Launch Calendar
  └─ Days 1-7: foundation (waitlist page, social profiles, press kit)
  └─ Days 8-14: audience building (Reddit, Twitter, LinkedIn content)
  └─ Days 15-21: affiliate + press outreach
  └─ Days 22-27: urgency building (countdown, founder story, social proof)
  └─ Days 28-30: launch sequence (Product Hunt, email blast, social storm)
        ↓
STEP 5 — Claude AI Writes All Launch Copy
  └─ Waitlist landing page (headline, subhead, bullets, CTA)
  └─ 5-email pre-launch sequence (build relationship before ask)
  └─ 3-email launch sequence (announce, follow-up, last chance)
  └─ 7 social posts (Twitter/LinkedIn — daily pre-launch content)
  └─ Product Hunt listing (tagline, description, first comment)
  └─ PR pitch (journalist-facing, 150 words max)
        ↓
STEP 6 — Affiliate & Partner Recruitment
  └─ Find creators + newsletters + podcasters already reaching your audience
  └─ Score by: audience size + relevance + engagement quality
  └─ Generate personalized outreach per affiliate
  └─ Commission structure recommendation
        ↓
STEP 7 — PR Target Identification
  └─ Journalists who covered similar products in last 12 months
  └─ Newsletters in your niche with 10K+ subscribers
  └─ Podcast hosts who interview founders in your space
  └─ Personalized pitch per press contact
        ↓
STEP 8 — InVideo AI Produces Launch Videos
  └─ 60-second demo/hype video for landing page + Product Hunt
  └─ 30-second social teaser for pre-launch buzz
  └─ These videos are the #1 asset that gets press + affiliate interest
        ↓
OUTPUT: Launch intelligence + positioning + 30-day calendar + all copy + affiliate list + PR targets + videos
```
