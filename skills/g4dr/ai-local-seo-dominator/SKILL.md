## ⚙️ The Local SEO Ranking Formula

```
THE 5 PILLARS OF LOCAL SEO DOMINANCE:

📍 PILLAR 1 — Google Business Profile (35% of ranking)
  └─ Complete profile: all categories, attributes, services filled
  └─ Photos: 10+ high-quality photos (businesses with photos get 42% more clicks)
  └─ Posts: weekly GBP posts signal active business to Google
  └─ Q&A: answered questions + keywords injected naturally
  └─ Video: GBP listings with video rank 53% higher

⭐ PILLAR 2 — Reviews (20% of ranking)
  └─ Total review count vs competitors
  └─ Review velocity: getting reviews consistently beats getting many at once
  └─ Response rate: responding to ALL reviews signals engagement to Google
  └─ Keyword-rich reviews: "best dentist in Austin" in a review = direct ranking signal

🏷️ PILLAR 3 — Citations & NAP Consistency (15% of ranking)
  └─ NAP: Name, Address, Phone must be IDENTICAL across all directories
  └─ Citation count vs competitors: how many directories list you?
  └─ Missing directories: Yelp, YellowPages, Bing Places, Apple Maps, etc.
  └─ Inconsistent NAP = ranking killer — Google can't trust your location data

🌐 PILLAR 4 — Website Local Signals (20% of ranking)
  └─ Title tags: "[Service] in [City]" on every page
  └─ Local schema markup: tells Google your exact location + hours
  └─ Location pages: one page per service area (if serving multiple cities)
  └─ Locally relevant content: blog posts mentioning local landmarks + neighborhoods

🔗 PILLAR 5 — Local Backlinks (10% of ranking)
  └─ Local news mentions: most powerful local signal
  └─ Chamber of Commerce listing
  └─ Local event sponsorships: get mentioned on event websites
  └─ Supplier/partner links: other local businesses linking to you
```
