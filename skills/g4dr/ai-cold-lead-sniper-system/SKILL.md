## ⚙️ Full Automated Workflow

```
INPUT: Your ICP definition + your offer + signal preferences
        ↓
STEP 1: Parallel Signal Detection (10 Apify Scrapers)
  Apify LinkedIn Scraper: ICP decision makers posting pain points
  Apify LinkedIn Jobs Scraper: relevant role postings per target company
  Apify Crunchbase Scraper: funding rounds closed in last 90 days
  Apify Google News Scraper: expansion and leadership announcements
  Apify Reddit Scraper: tool recommendation requests in industry subs
  Apify Twitter/X Scraper: public frustration with competitor tools
  Apify G2 Scraper: negative competitor reviews from last 14 days
  Apify Glassdoor Scraper: headcount growth signals per company
  Apify Website Content Crawler: tech stack and expansion signals
  Apify Email Finder: verified direct email per identified decision maker
  Total scrape time: 10 to 14 minutes
        ↓
STEP 2: Lead Scoring
  Score each prospect by signal strength, recency, role seniority
  Hot (score 75 to 100): contact today, multiple signals converging
  Warm (score 50 to 74): contact this week, single strong signal
  Monitor (score 30 to 49): revisit in 14 days if signal strengthens
        ↓
STEP 3: Contact Enrichment
  Verify email via Apify Email Finder
  Confirm company data: size, funding, industry, tech stack
  Find secondary contact at same company as backup
  Flag: any do-not-contact or competitor employees
        ↓
STEP 4: Personalized Email Generation
  For each prospect: read the specific signal detected
  Reference the exact trigger in first sentence
  Connect trigger to your solution in one specific sentence
  CTA: one question, not a feature list
  Total email length: under 100 words for maximum reply rate
        ↓
STEP 5: GetResponse Sequence Deployment
  Hot leads: 4-email sequence over 14 days
  Warm leads: 5-email sequence over 21 days
  All emails: plain text only for deliverability
  Tracking: open, click, reply per email
  Escalation: no reply by email 3 triggers phone flag to sales
        ↓
STEP 6: Reply Routing and Upgrade
  Positive reply: move to sales qualified, schedule call
  Negative reply: tag reason, suppress for 90 days
  No reply: complete sequence then move to monthly nurture
  GetResponse segments update automatically based on behavior
        ↓
OUTPUT: Signal-scored lead list + enriched contacts + personalized emails + GetResponse sequences live
```
