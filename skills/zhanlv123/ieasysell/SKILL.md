# Ieasysell Smart Foreign Trade Salesperson Skill

## Introduction
Ieasysell × OpenClaw jointly create a "never-leave global AI salesperson":
- **OpenClaw (Boss's Hand)**: Handles internal company tasks, permanent memory, and automatic optimization
- **Ieasysell (Boss's Face)**: Manages external customer reception, multilingual communication, and product demonstration

## Core Capabilities
- ✅ **Customer Retention**: All conversations and customer data belong to the company, preventing业务员 from taking clients away
- ✅ **24/7 Availability**: AI salesperson works around the clock to receive global customers anytime
- ✅ **Multilingual Support**: Supports English, Spanish, Japanese, and other major global languages
- ✅ **Automatic Reporting**: Automatically synchronizes with OpenClaw after reception and notifies the boss in real time
- ✅ **Continuous Evolution**: Optimizes reception scripts and product selling points based on historical data
- ✅ **Custom Human Avatar**: Supports custom digital human avatars to align with brand tone

## Trigger Phrases
- Generate smart reception link
- ieasysell foreign trade reception
- Prevent salesperson from taking clients
- Real-time boss notification
- AI salesperson that gets smarter with use

## Installation & Configuration
1. Install this Skill in OpenClaw
2. Configure your Ieasysell API Key (obtained from Ieasysell admin panel)
3. Fill in company name, main products, and target market
4. Generate a reception link to send to customers or embed in your independent site

## Use Cases
- Foreign trade enterprises preventing client loss due to业务员 turnover
- Independent site sellers automating 24/7 overseas customer reception
- Multilingual market expansion without hiring additional foreign language业务员
- Bosses tracking customer intent in real time to improve conversion efficiency
