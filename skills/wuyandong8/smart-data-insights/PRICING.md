# 💰 Pricing Strategy

## Overview

The Data Analyst Skill is available in multiple tiers to suit different needs and budgets.

## Pricing Tiers

### 🆓 Free Tier

**Price**: $0 forever

**Features**:
- ✅ Basic data analysis
- ✅ Data cleaning (up to 100 rows)
- ✅ Simple visualizations (3 charts)
- ✅ Summary reports
- ✅ Community support

**Limitations**:
- ❌ Max 100 rows per analysis
- ❌ Max 3 charts per analysis
- ❌ Basic report format only
- ❌ No priority support

**Best for**: Students, hobbyists, small projects

---

### 💎 Pro Tier

**Price**: $29/month or $290/year (save 17%)

**Features**:
- ✅ Everything in Free
- ✅ Unlimited rows
- ✅ Unlimited charts
- ✅ Full report generation
- ✅ Advanced cleaning strategies
- ✅ Custom visualizations
- ✅ Export to PDF/HTML
- ✅ Email support (24h response)
- ✅ Monthly updates

**Best for**: Freelancers, small businesses, analysts

---

### 🏢 Enterprise Tier

**Price**: Custom (starting at $499/month)

**Features**:
- ✅ Everything in Pro
- ✅ Priority support (4h response)

- ✅ SSO integration
- ✅ Dedicated account manager
