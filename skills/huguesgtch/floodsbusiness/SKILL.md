# Floods — Business Model & Product Knowledge Base

## What Floods Is

Floods is a bootstrapped US LLC operating large-scale organic short-form distribution networks. The company controls a centralized network of third-party short-form video accounts generating approximately **5 billion impressions per month** across TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

Floods sells **CPM-based organic impressions** with geo and demographic targeting.

It is **not** an influencer marketing agency. It is **not** an ad network. It is **not** a content studio. It is **distribution infrastructure for the organic feed**.

---

## How It Works — The 3-Step Process

### Step 1: Brief

The client defines their campaign parameters:

- Target impressions (e.g., 500M+)
- Gender focus (Male / Female)
- Age range (e.g., 18–34)
- Geos (e.g., US, FR, BR, UK)
- Platform targets (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube)
- Brand integrity rules

Floods provides the exact CPM rate during the briefing phase. The client sets the budget; Floods delivers the impressions.

### Step 2: Flood

Floods handles content production, account allocation, and deployment. Content featuring the client's game is distributed across the verified account network — blending into the organic feed as entertainment content, not ads.

- **Launch time:** 7–14 business days from brief to first impressions
- **Zero client dev work required:** no SDK integration, no attribution stack changes
- Plugs into the client's existing UA operation without touching their tech stack

### Step 3: Results

Impact is tracked through two layers:

1. **Direct attribution** via link-in-bio tracking (every account has a tracked link to the app store page)
2. **Organic lift** on existing paid UA metrics (the primary value driver)

## Pricing Model

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| Model | Fixed CPM — no auction, no bidding, no hidden fees |
| Half-screen split content (standard format) | ~$0.80 CPM |
| Paid social benchmark (Meta / TikTok Ads) | $15–25 CPM |
| Cost advantage | 20–25× cheaper than paid social |
| Billing basis | Verified human views only — bot traffic excluded before billing |
| Delivery guarantee | Client sets budget; Floods delivers the impressions |

Exact rates are provided during the briefing phase and vary by content format and geo mix.

---

## Impression Verification — 3-Layer Process

**Layer 1 — Pre-campaign:** Every account in the network is audited before any campaign starts.

**Layer 2 — During delivery:** Real-time bot detection runs on 100% of views throughout the campaign.

**Layer 3 — Post-campaign:** Invalid traffic is filtered and excluded from billing. Client receives a full delivery report with:
- Geo breakdown
- Platform split
- Demographic data

Floods only bills **net verified human impressions**.

---

## Attribution Model

### Layer 1: Direct Attribution (Link-in-Bio)

Every account in a campaign carries a tracked link pointing to the client's app store page. Floods measures installs directly through these links.

### Layer 2: Organic Lift on Paid UA (Primary Value)

When users have seen the client's game 15+ times in their organic feed, paid ads perform structurally better. This is the core thesis.

Demonstrated impact metrics (validated, from live campaigns):

| Metric | Before | After | Delta |
|---|---|---|---|
| CPI | $4.20 | $2.80 | ↓ 33% |
| CTR | 1.2% | 2.1% | ↑ 75% |
| ROAS | 1.4× | 2.3× | ↑ 64% |

The client measures lift in their own dashboards using their existing attribution stack. No new tooling required.

## Brand Safety & Quality Assurance

Before any campaign starts, Floods collects the client's brand guidelines — tone, visual identity, do's and don'ts. Every content format is reviewed against those guidelines before publishing.

- Real-time content monitoring runs throughout the campaign
- Any non-compliant piece is taken down immediately
- Client has **full takedown authority at any time**

Floods has delivered billions of impressions for compliance-sensitive brands. This is infrastructure, not influencer marketing — the client retains control.

## Game Types Supported

All genres. The format adapts to any game type:

- **UA-heavy strategy titles** (spending $1M–100M+/month on paid): organic acts as a direct multiplier on existing spend
- **Mid-core competitive games** (Brawl Stars, Clash Royale): community and organic discovery drive long-term growth
- **Casual titles** (Royal Match, Homescapes, Gossip Harbor): entertainment-native content is exactly what the target audience consumes

Genres supported: strategy, RPG, puzzle, merge, match-3, action, board games, and more.

**Rule of thumb:** if the game has gameplay footage, Floods can build a distribution campaign around it.

## Floods vs. Influencer Marketing — Key Distinctions

| Dimension | Influencer Marketing | Floods |
|---|---|---|
| Model | Pay individual creators who own their audience | Own the distribution infrastructure directly |
| Scale | Hard to scale; unpredictable per-creator | Thousands of verified accounts, billions of impressions |
| Control | Creator controls content, timing, tone | Full control over content, targeting, frequency, quality, compliance |
| Pricing | Expensive, variable, negotiated per deal | Fixed CPM, guaranteed delivery |
| Nature | Talent relationship management | Performance-based media buying for the organic feed |

**Mental model for clients:** think Meta Ads logic applied to organic distribution.
