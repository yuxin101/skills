- **Key light**: Natural window light or equivalent

- **Accent light**: Practical lights in scene

- White balance: Warm (4500-5000K)
- Saturation: Natural to slightly enhanced
- Contrast: Moderate
- Highlights: Soft, natural
- Shadows: Open, detailed
