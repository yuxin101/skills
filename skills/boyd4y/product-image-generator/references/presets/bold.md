## Lighting Setup

- **Key light**: Hard light source, direct
- **Fill light**: Minimal, maintain contrast
- **Accent light**: Colored gels optional
- **Background**: Bold color, evenly lit

## Color Treatment

- White balance: Cool or warm (depending on color scheme)
- Saturation: Enhanced, vibrant
- Contrast: High
- Highlights: Bright, punchy
- Shadows: Deep, defined
