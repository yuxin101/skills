## Lighting Setup

- **Key light**: Large softbox, very diffused
- **Fill light**: Ample, open shadows
- **Accent light**: Soft backlight optional
- **Background**: Soft pastel colors

## Color Treatment

- White balance: Warm (4000-4500K)
- Saturation: Muted, pastel
- Contrast: Low-medium
- Highlights: Soft, glowing
- Shadows: Open, minimal
