## Lighting Setup

- **Key light**: Large softbox, 45 degrees
- **Fill light**: Reflect card, opposite side
- **Background**: Evenly lit, pure white
- **Intensity**: Balanced, no harsh shadows

## Color Treatment

- White balance: Neutral (5500K)
- Saturation: Natural, no enhancement
- Contrast: Moderate
- Highlights: Controlled
- Shadows: Minimal, clean
