## Lighting Setup

- **Key light**: Focused, dramatic
- **Fill light**: Minimal, maintain contrast
- **Accent light**: Strategic highlights on product
- **Background**: Deep rich colors, velvet-like

## Color Treatment

- White balance: Slightly warm (4800K)
- Saturation: Rich, deep
- Contrast: High
- Highlights: Precise, controlled
- Shadows: Deep, mysterious
