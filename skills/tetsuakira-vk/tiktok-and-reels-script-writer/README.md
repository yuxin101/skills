# TikTok and Reels Script Writer — OpenClaw Skill

**Ready-to-film TikTok and Instagram Reels scripts in three proven formats.**

Stop staring at a blank page before you film. Give it a topic, get a complete script with hooks, spoken words, on-screen text cues, and captions.

---

## What it does

Tell it your topic and format. Get back a complete script including:

- **Spoken words** — word for word, written for delivery not reading
- **On-screen text overlays** — timed, max 6 words each
- **Pacing notes** — where to slow down, speed up, or pause for effect
- **End hook** — a comment-driving question or statement
- **Caption** — with hashtag block of 15–20 tags

Three formats available:

| Format | Best for | Length |
|---|---|---|
| Trending audio hook | Product showcases, aesthetic content, mood-driven videos | 20–45 sec |
| Story/narrative | Personal experiences, case studies, transformations | 45–90 sec |
| Educational / did you know | Facts, insights, explainers, history | 30–60 sec |

Ask for one format or all three at once.

---

## Who it's for

- Content creators who film regularly but hate scripting
- Brands and small businesses creating social content
- Podcasters and writers repurposing their content for short video
- Anyone who knows what they want to say but not how to say it in 60 seconds

## Usage

```
Use tiktok-script-writer for: [topic] in [format] format
```

Examples:
```
Use tiktok-script-writer for: the unsolved DB Cooper hijacking in educational format
Use tiktok-script-writer for: my weight loss journey in story format
Use tiktok-script-writer for: this Japanese language app in hook format
Use tiktok-script-writer for: Japanese history facts — give me all three formats
```

---

## Features

- Three distinct formats with different structures, not just tone variations
- Every script front-loads the hook — optimised for the first 3 seconds
- On-screen text cues included with timing notes
- Caption written separately from the script — optimised for the feed preview
- Hashtag blocks included, mix of niche and broad
- Suggests splitting into a series if the topic is too big for one video

## Tips

- The more specific your topic the better the hook — "a weird fact about Japan" beats "Japanese culture"
- Use the educational format for repurposing podcast or blog content
- Run all three formats on a topic and pick the one that fits your style
- Save scripts you like and ask for variations: "Give me 3 alternative hooks for this script"
