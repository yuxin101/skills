## Project Title
Global Nuclear Brief

## Summary (short)
An OpenClaw skill that turns live nuclear energy policy/regulatory updates into a grounded, decision‑ready brief for analysts.

## Problem
Policy teams and energy organizations must track fast‑moving nuclear policy/regulatory changes across many sources. Manual tracking is slow, inconsistent, and error‑prone.

## Solution
Global Nuclear Brief automates monitoring with Apify and synthesizes a structured brief using Contextual, grounded only in retrieved sources. It outputs what changed, why it matters, risks, and recommended actions.

## Sponsor tools used
- Apify (live data extraction)
- Contextual (grounded generation)

## Demo workflow
1. Pull live nuclear policy/regulatory news (Apify)
2. Generate a structured brief from those sources (Contextual)
3. Show the brief and the sources list

## What the tools enabled
- Apify: live, current sources instead of canned data
- Contextual: grounded summaries that cite the retrieved sources
