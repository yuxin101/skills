# CORE-EEAT Content Quality Benchmark

This benchmark defines 80 content quality audit criteria across 8 core dimensions. These standards are used to evaluate content's GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) potential and SEO (Search Engine Optimization) strength.

## Benchmark Overview

### CORE — Content Quality

CORE evaluates the quality of content itself, focusing on:

- **C (Contextual Clarity)**: Is the content clear, accurate, and does it directly answer user questions?
- **O (Organization)**: Does the content have good structure, hierarchy, and navigability?
- **R (Referenceability)**: Does the content have sufficient data, evidence, and citations to support claims?
- **E (Exclusivity)**: Does the content offer unique insights, data, and perspectives?

### EEAT — Source Credibility

EEAT evaluates the credibility of the content source, focusing on:

- **Exp (Experience)**: Does the author demonstrate actual usage experience and real testing?
- **Ept (Expertise)**: Does the author demonstrate professional knowledge and industry skills?
- **A (Authority)**: Does the content source possess authority and industry status?
- **T (Trust)**: Is the content trustworthy, with transparency and credibility?

### GEO vs SEO

- **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)**: Optimized for AI search engines (e.g., Google SGE, Bing Chat), emphasizing direct answer capability, referenceability, and exclusivity
- **SEO (Search Engine Optimization)**: Optimized for traditional search engines, emphasizing source credibility, authority, and professionalism

## CORE Dimensions Detail

### C — Contextual Clarity

**Goal**: Ensure content is clear, accurate, and directly answers user questions

#### C01 Intent Alignment 🎯 GEO-First
**Definition**: Content must match user search intent
**Standards**:
- Title accurately reflects content topic
- First paragraph clearly promises content value
- Body fully delivers on title and first paragraph promises
- No clickbait or misleading statements
**Scoring**:
- Pass: Intent fully aligned, content matches search intent
- Partial: Basically aligned but with minor deviations
- Fail: Intent not aligned, content doesn't match promise (triggers veto)

#### C02 Direct Answer 🎯 GEO-First
**Definition**: Provide direct, clear answers at content beginning
**Standards**:
- Core answer included within first 300 words
- Prioritize conclusions or key information
- Avoid excessive preamble or lengthy introductions
**Scoring**:
- Pass: Clear answer or summary box within first 150 words
- Partial: Answer present within first 300 words but not prominently placed
- Fail: Answer buried deep in content or no clear answer

#### C03 Query Coverage 🎯 GEO-First
**Definition**: Cover derived queries related to main query
**Standards**:
- Identify and cover relevant long-tail queries
- Answer implicit related questions
- Provide answers to questions users might ask next
**Scoring**:
- Pass: Covers 80%+ relevant queries
- Partial: Covers 50-80% relevant queries
- Fail: Relevant query coverage below 50%

#### C04 Definition First
**Definition**: Provide definitions when using professional terms for the first time
**Standards**:
- Define key terms immediately upon first use
- Definitions concise and accurate
- Avoid jargon overload
**Scoring**:
- Pass: All key terms have clear definitions
- Partial: Main terms have definitions, some missing
- Fail: Key terms undefined or definitions unclear

#### C05 Topic Scope
**Definition**: Clearly define content's topic scope
**Standards**:
- Clearly state what content covers
- Clearly state what content does not cover
- Avoid over-promising
**Scoring**:
- Pass: Topic scope clearly defined (includes/excludes)
- Partial: States what's covered but not what's excluded
- Fail: Topic scope unclear

#### C06 Audience Targeting
**Definition**: Clearly identify content's target audience
**Standards**:
- Clearly state target audience
- Content language and depth match audience level
- Avoid audience mismatch
**Scoring**:
- Pass: Audience clear, content matches
- Partial: Has audience targeting but slight depth mismatch
- Fail: Audience unclear or content doesn't match

#### C07 Semantic Coherence
**Definition**: Content flows logically with coherent viewpoints
**Standards**:
- Logical transitions between paragraphs
- Arguments progress layer by layer
- No abrupt jumps or breaks
**Scoring**:
- Pass: Logical flow, natural transitions
- Partial: Basically coherent but some stiff transitions
- Fail: Confused logic, lacks coherence

#### C08 Use Case Mapping
**Definition**: Content covers specific usage scenarios or application scenarios
**Standards**:
- Provide specific use cases or scenarios
- Use cases highly relevant to topic
- Help users understand actual application
**Scoring**:
- Pass: Provides multiple specific use cases covering main scenarios
- Partial: Has use cases but incomplete coverage
- Fail: Lacks specific use cases or irrelevant

#### C09 FAQ Coverage 🎯 GEO-First
**Definition**: Answer common questions users might ask
**Standards**:
- Identify and answer common questions
- FAQ section independent and clear
- Questions targeted, not generic
**Scoring**:
- Pass: Has FAQ section answering 5+ related questions
- Partial: Has FAQ but fewer than 5 or average quality
- Fail: No FAQ section

#### C10 Semantic Closure
**Definition**: Conclusion ties back to opening, forming complete loop
**Standards**:
- Conclusion summarizes core points
- Ties back to title and opening promises
- Avoid abrupt endings
**Scoring**:
- Pass: Ending fully ties back to opening, forms loop
- Partial: Has conclusion but doesn't fully tie back to opening
- Fail: Abrupt or missing ending

## Veto Item Definitions

The following three are "one-vote veto items"; once triggered regardless of total score, content is considered to have serious issues:

### T04: Disclosure Statement ⚠️ Veto Item
**Trigger Condition**: Content sponsored, has conflicts of interest, or partnerships but not clearly disclosed in content
**Consequence**: Directly judged as untrustworthy content
**Recommendation**: Immediately add disclosure banner at top of page clearly stating sponsorship, conflicts of interest, or partnerships

### C01: Intent Alignment ⚠️ Veto Item
**Trigger Condition**: Title promises content that doesn't match actual body content, misleading
**Consequence**: Extremely poor user experience, reduced trust
**Recommendation**: Rewrite title and first paragraph to ensure complete alignment with body content

### R10: Content Consistency ⚠️ Veto Item
**Trigger Condition**: Internal content data, claims, or conclusions have contradictions
**Consequence**: Content credibility seriously damaged
**Recommendation**: Verify all data, eliminate contradictions, ensure content前后consistent
