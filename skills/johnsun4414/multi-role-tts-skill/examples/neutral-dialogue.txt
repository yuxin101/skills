# Multi-role TTS Skill Test Script

RoleA: Hello, I am Role A, your assistant.
RoleB: I am Role B, here to support you.
Narrator: Narrator ready, starting recording.

RoleA: Today we will discuss the project plan.
RoleB: Project planning is important, I'll help throughout.
Narrator: Environment check complete, all systems ready.

RoleA: Let's review the timeline first.
RoleB: The timeline looks good, we're on schedule.
Narrator: Discussion progressing, notes being taken.

RoleA: Next, let's check the budget allocation.
RoleB: Budget is within limits, good management.
Narrator: Financial review complete, all approved.

RoleA: Any other items to discuss?
RoleB: I think we covered everything important.
Narrator: Meeting summary: productive discussion.

# End
RoleA: Thank you for your participation.
RoleB: Looking forward to next meeting.
Narrator: Recording complete, meeting ended.
