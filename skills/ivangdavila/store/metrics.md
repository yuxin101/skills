# Weekly Metrics Review

## Core Numbers

| Metric | What It Tells You | Typical Follow-Up |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Sales | demand captured | compare against traffic and promo mix |
| Traffic | opportunity volume | compare against conversion |
| Conversion | selling effectiveness | check service coverage and floor clarity |
| Average ticket | basket value | review bundles and add-on behavior |
| Gross margin | quality of revenue | check discounting and mix |
| Labor hours | staffing input | compare against traffic and sales |
| Stock-outs | lost demand | fix replenishment and ordering |
| Shrink | operating leakage | investigate controls and process |

## Weekly Review Sequence

1. What improved?
2. What deteriorated?
3. Which problem is biggest in money or customer impact?
4. What single action matters most next week?
5. Who owns it and how will success be checked?

## Interpreting Patterns

- Sales up + conversion down -> traffic saved the week, service may be weaker.
- Sales flat + ticket up -> fewer customers, stronger basket.
- Margin down + sales up -> promotion or discount mix may be carrying volume.
- Labor flat + traffic up -> team productivity improved or service quality is being stretched.
- Stock-outs high + promo active -> offer execution or replenishment failed.

## Hard Rules

- Do not celebrate sales wins that came from margin collapse.
- Do not blame staff before checking traffic mix, stock position, and promo setup.
- Do not review metrics without naming the action they imply.
- Do not carry more than one major operational priority into the next week.
