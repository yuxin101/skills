# Tech Ecosystem - Thailand

## Market Overview

Bangkok is the primary technology and startup center with the strongest concentration of product teams, regional HQ operations, and enterprise buyers.

## Secondary Tech Bases

- Chiang Mai: smaller but active remote and indie scene.
- Phuket: tourism-tech and lifestyle-business opportunities.

## Role Patterns

- Product, growth, and operations roles cluster in Bangkok.
- Cross-border APAC coordination roles are common in multinational teams.
- Remote contractor and founder-operator models are increasingly common.

## Salary and Cost Logic

- Nominal pay may be lower than top global hubs.
- Net lifestyle value can remain strong depending on city and housing choices.
- Premium urban living can erase expected savings.

## Strategic Advice

- Match city choice to career objective: salary growth, startup velocity, or lifestyle balance.
- Use Bangkok for network-intensive phases; use secondary bases for focus phases.
