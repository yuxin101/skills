# Culture and Etiquette - Thailand

## Core Social Norms

- Respectful, calm communication is highly valued.
- Public confrontation and visible anger damage outcomes.
- Seniority and social context matter in many environments.

## Temple and Sacred-Space Etiquette

- Dress modestly for temples and formal religious spaces.
- Remove shoes where expected.
- Treat sacred imagery and rituals with visible respect.

## Legal-Sensitivity Context

- Monarchy-related speech and content require strict caution.
- Online posts can create legal and social consequences.

## Practical Advice for Newcomers

- Use polite tone and low-friction behavior in service interactions.
- Avoid assuming direct Western communication style is always effective.
- Ask before photographing people in culturally sensitive contexts.

## Festival Awareness

- Songkran and Loy Krathong have major operational effects on mobility, crowds, and bookings.
