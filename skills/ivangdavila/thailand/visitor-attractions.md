# Visitor Attractions - Thailand

## Bangkok Core

### High value
- Grand Palace and Wat Phra Kaew: go early, strict dress, avoid midday heat.
- Wat Arun + river crossing: best in late afternoon for light and lower heat.
- Jim Thompson House + nearby Siam museums for a compact culture block.
- Chinatown (Yaowarat) after sunset for food density and atmosphere.

### Often overhyped for short stays
- Oversized shopping-only days in distant malls if user came for culture.
- Long queue attractions with no prebooked slot in peak season.

## Northern Thailand

### Chiang Mai and nearby
- Old City temples plus neighborhood cafes and local markets.
- Doi Suthep at sunrise or late afternoon, not peak midday.
- Day trips to Doi Inthanon or Chiang Rai only with realistic drive times.

### Pai and mountain routes
- Works for slow travel profiles, not for tight first-time itineraries.
- Road curves are intense; motion-sensitive users should avoid scooter-heavy plans.

## Andaman Coast

### Phuket base
- Combine one cultural day (Old Town) with selected beaches.
- Use early boat departures for island trips and weather buffers.

### Krabi and nearby
- Railay, Hong islands, and Phi Phi are best in shoulder windows.
- Avoid overstacked speedboat programs in rough-season forecasts.

## Gulf Coast and Islands

### Samui, Phangan, Tao
- Samui: best full-service island base.
- Phangan: mixed wellness plus nightlife profile.
- Tao: diving-first travelers.

## Cultural Priority Experiences

- Temple visits with correct attire and behavior.
- Night markets with local-food focus by region.
- Festival periods (Songkran, Loy Krathong) with crowd and safety planning.
- Thai cooking classes in trusted schools, especially for longer stays.

## Profile Matching

| Profile | Priority attractions |
|--------|----------------------|
| First-time 7 days | Bangkok highlights + one secondary region |
| Food-focused | Bangkok Chinatown, Chiang Mai markets, southern seafood zones |
| Nature-focused | Northern parks + island day trips with buffer days |
| Family | Bangkok museums, ethical animal parks, easy-access beaches |
| Luxury | Bangkok premium stays + Phuket or Samui resort clusters |

## Trap Avoidance

- Do not stack three long-distance transfers in one week.
- Do not put temple-heavy plans into hottest midday windows.
- Do not book non-refundable island tours in unstable weather periods.
