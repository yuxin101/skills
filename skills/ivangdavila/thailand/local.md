# Local Services - Thailand

## Essential Apps

- Maps and navigation.
- Ride-hailing and food delivery.
- Airline and rail apps for domestic movement.
- Translation and local-payment support where available.

## Connectivity Setup

- Airport or city-center SIM/eSIM from major providers.
- Validate speed and hotspot rules for remote work use cases.
- Keep dual-SIM options for users moving across regions frequently.

## Banking and Payments

- International cards are accepted in major zones, but cash still matters.
- ATM fee behavior varies by bank and card type.
- QR payment ecosystems are strong but can be non-resident constrained.

## Admin Practicalities

- Keep passport and visa records accessible and backed up.
- Track key deadlines for visa reporting or extension windows.
- Keep lease, deposit, and payment evidence documented.
