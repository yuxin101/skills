# Street Food Playbook - Thailand

## What Works Best

- High-turnover vendors with visible prep and clean stations.
- Evening markets in Bangkok, Chiang Mai, and key coastal zones.
- Breakfast and lunch street clusters near local business districts.

## Safety Heuristics

- Prefer cooked-to-order hot dishes.
- Avoid long-exposed seafood in high heat.
- Watch water and ice assumptions if user has sensitive digestion.

## Budget Reality

- Core street meals often THB 50-120.
- Premium market dishes and seafood can scale above THB 200-400.

## Good User Prompts

- "I want local food with low risk."
- "I need vegetarian Thai options near BTS lines."
- "I need halal-friendly options in Bangkok and Phuket."

## Trap List

- Choosing lines based only on social media without freshness checks.
- Ignoring region-specific spice intensity.
- Treating all market stalls as equal hygiene level.
