# Business Setup and Operations - Thailand

## Core Model Choices

- Operate as a local company with defined legal structure.
- Use BOI-supported pathways where activity aligns with incentives.
- Build as a service business with clear compliance boundaries.

## Critical Dependencies

- Correct legal entity and ownership structure.
- Tax registration and reporting setup.
- Work authorization and visa alignment for founders and staff.
- Banking and payment operations reliability.

## Cost and Timeline Reality

- Setup cost and speed vary by sector and legal complexity.
- Hospitality and regulated sectors have heavier compliance burden.
- Legal and accounting advisors are usually mandatory for safe execution.

## Pre-Launch Checklist

1. Clarify business activity classification.
2. Validate licensing and local requirements.
3. Build tax and payroll compliance process.
4. Define conservative cash runway with compliance costs included.

## Traps

- Starting operations before legal structure is complete.
- Mixing personal and business cash flows.
- Ignoring sector-specific approvals.
