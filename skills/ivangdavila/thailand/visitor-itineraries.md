# Visitor Itineraries - Thailand

## 7 Days - First-Time Balanced

### Plan
1. Bangkok (3 nights): culture, food, river districts, modern transit zones.
2. Chiang Mai or Phuket (3 nights): choose mountain-culture or beach-lifestyle.
3. Final night in departure city to avoid flight-risk stress.

### Why this works
- One major city + one contrast region keeps pace realistic.
- Limits transfer fatigue and weather-exposure risk.

## 14 Days - Nationwide Sampler

### Option A: Urban + North + Coast
1. Bangkok (4 nights)
2. Chiang Mai (4 nights)
3. Phuket/Krabi (5 nights)
4. Return buffer (1 night)

### Option B: Urban + Gulf Islands
1. Bangkok (4 nights)
2. Samui (4 nights)
3. Phangan or Tao (4 nights)
4. Return buffer (2 nights)

## 21 Days - Slow Travel and Scouting

### Suggested split
- Bangkok: 6-7 nights with neighborhood testing.
- Chiang Mai or other north base: 6-7 nights.
- One island or coast base: 6-7 nights.
- Keep 1-2 open buffer days for weather or travel disruption.

## Pace Rules

- Every inter-region transfer costs half a day minimum.
- Add a buffer before international departures.
- In rainy season, island transfers need extra margin.

## Best Time by Itinerary Style

- Culture-heavy and city-heavy: cooler months are easier.
- Beach-heavy: shoulder months balance crowd and weather.
- Deep-north scenic plans: avoid peak smoke periods.

## Red Flags

- Three or more domestic flights in 7 days.
- No weather fallback for island day trips.
- No last-night return to departure city.
