# Food Overview - Thailand

## National Food Reality

Thailand combines strong local-value food systems with premium global dining in major hubs. Food strategy should be location-specific, not generic.

## Regional Differences

| Region | Flavor and dining pattern |
|--------|---------------------------|
| Central/Bangkok | Broadest range from street food to global fine dining |
| North | Herb-heavy dishes, milder heat options, slower dining rhythm |
| Northeast (Isan) | Bold acidity, grilled meats, fermented and spicy profiles |
| South | Seafood density, stronger spice and turmeric profiles |

## Dining Modes

- Street-first value mode.
- Casual modern Thai restaurants.
- Premium and chef-driven restaurants in Bangkok and Phuket.
- International clusters in expat-heavy areas.

## Practical Strategy

- Use street food where turnover is high and prep is visible.
- For dietary complexity, pre-select restaurants with clear labeling.
- Reserve premium tables early in peak seasons and weekends.
