# Healthcare and Insurance - Thailand

## System Landscape

Thailand has strong private-hospital options in major cities, with quality variance across regions.

## Where care is strongest

- Bangkok: broadest specialist depth and major international hospitals.
- Phuket and Chiang Mai: good private options, fewer ultra-specialist pathways.
- Secondary provinces: basic and moderate care options, referral planning needed.

## Insurance Strategy

- Confirm inpatient/outpatient coverage and network hospitals.
- Check emergency evacuation or intercity transfer rules for island users.
- Verify pre-existing condition handling before relocation.

## Family and Long-Stay Users

- Map nearest emergency and pediatric care options.
- Keep bilingual contact list for urgent care use.
- Build annual preventive-care workflow.

## Common Mistakes

- Choosing location with no nearby quality emergency care.
- Assuming all private hospitals accept the same insurance terms.
- Under-insuring while planning long stays.
