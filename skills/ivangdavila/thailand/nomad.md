# Digital Nomad and Remote Work - Thailand

## Where It Works Best

- Bangkok: strongest global-infrastructure and network effects.
- Chiang Mai: cost-efficient and focus-friendly for long stays.
- Phuket/Samui: lifestyle-forward with higher costs.

## Remote Work Checklist

- Reliable home internet and mobile fallback.
- Time-zone schedule fit with team obligations.
- Legal posture for stay and work activity.
- Health and burnout management in tropical climate.

## Budget Profiles

| Style | Typical monthly range |
|------|------------------------|
| Lean remote setup | THB 35,000-65,000 |
| Comfortable mid-range | THB 65,000-130,000 |
| Premium lifestyle | THB 130,000+ |

## Work Environment Trade-offs

- Bangkok: productivity + networking, higher stress/cost.
- Chiang Mai: focus + value, less large-client density.
- Islands: lifestyle quality, weaker reliability for tight schedules.

## Red Flags

- No legal clarity on stay/work framework.
- No backup internet for client-critical workloads.
- Choosing base only for aesthetics, not workflow reality.
