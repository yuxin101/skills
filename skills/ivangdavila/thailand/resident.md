# Resident and Relocation Guide - Thailand

## First 60 Days Priorities

1. Select base city using work, healthcare, and legal constraints.
2. Pilot neighborhood before annual lease commitment.
3. Set up connectivity, payment flows, and transport routines.
4. Validate visa/reporting requirements and deadline workflow.

## Housing Strategy

- Short pilot stay first.
- Validate noise, flood history, internet, and commute.
- Confirm deposit terms and exit clauses in writing.

## Family Relocation Priorities

- School admissions calendar and transport load.
- Healthcare network fit with insurance.
- Daily logistics under heat and traffic constraints.

## Solo and Couple Relocation Priorities

- Work-friendly environment fit.
- Social and support network accessibility.
- Long-term legal and financial sustainability.

## Relocation Traps

- Committing to long lease before base validation.
- Using short-stay assumptions for year-round quality of life.
- Ignoring legal and compliance timeline details.
