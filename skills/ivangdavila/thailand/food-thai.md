# Thai Cuisine Essentials

## Core Dish Families

- Rice-based mains: stir-fries, curries, grilled proteins.
- Noodle systems: soups, stir-fried noodles, regional variants.
- Isan style: grilled meats, salads, bold chili-lime balance.
- Southern style: seafood-rich, stronger spice and coconut profiles.

## Region-Specific Notes

- Bangkok: widest cross-regional menu access.
- Chiang Mai: northern signatures and herb-rich profiles.
- Phuket and south: seafood and southern spice foundations.

## Customization Guidance

- Spice level should be explicitly negotiated.
- Fish sauce, shrimp paste, and stock bases matter for vegetarian requests.
- Allergy and dietary constraints need direct confirmation before ordering.

## Suggested User Flow

1. Confirm spice tolerance and restrictions.
2. Recommend dish cluster by region.
3. Suggest venue type and timing.
