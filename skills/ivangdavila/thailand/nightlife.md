# Nightlife and Social Zones - Thailand

## Bangkok

- Sukhumvit corridors: broad nightlife density.
- Silom and nearby zones: mixed social and club scenes.
- Riverside: premium bars and calmer evening formats.

## Chiang Mai

- Compact social zones, live music, and café-bar hybrids.
- Lower intensity than Bangkok nightlife districts.

## Phuket and Islands

- Patong and selected beach strips: high-intensity nightlife.
- Samui and Phangan: mixed party and wellness ecosystems.

## Practical Safety Rules

- Use licensed/app-based transport at night.
- Keep drink and payment control disciplined.
- Avoid high-risk substances and legal-gray behavior.
- Keep accommodation return route preplanned.
