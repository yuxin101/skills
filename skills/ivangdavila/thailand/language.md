# Thai Language and Communication Basics

## Practical Reality

English is common in tourism and premium business zones but inconsistent across regions and services. Basic Thai improves speed, pricing fairness, and daily trust.

## High-Value Language Use Cases

- Transport and directions.
- Food ordering and dietary clarification.
- Market and service negotiation.
- Administrative interactions outside tourist cores.

## Communication Strategy

- Keep phrases short and polite.
- Confirm numbers, dates, and times twice for critical bookings.
- Use translation apps as backup, not as sole strategy.

## Recommended Minimum Toolkit

- Essential greetings and courtesy phrases.
- Numbers and time expressions.
- Food and allergy vocabulary.
- Address and landmark pronunciation support.
