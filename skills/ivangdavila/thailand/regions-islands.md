# Regions - Gulf Islands and Secondary Coasts

## Primary Island Profiles

| Island | Best for | Friction |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Samui | Full-service island base, mixed traveler types | Higher price floor than mainland |
| Phangan | Wellness + social scene, flexible stay lengths | Party-zone noise and transfer reliance |
| Tao | Diving-first travelers and compact island life | Limited specialist services |

## Route Planning Guidance

- Keep ferry and flight buffers in rainy months.
- Do not schedule same-day international connections from island routes.
- Match island to purpose, not social-media aesthetics.

## Who Should Avoid Island-First Plans

- Users with frequent medical specialist needs.
- Users with fixed high-intensity meeting schedules.
- Families requiring predictable school-year routines.

## Best Use Cases

- Lifestyle sabbatical with schedule flexibility.
- Diving-focused short windows.
- Recovery or wellness blocks after urban work phases.
