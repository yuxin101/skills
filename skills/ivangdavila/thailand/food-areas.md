# Food Areas - Thailand

## Bangkok

- Chinatown (Yaowarat): dense evening food strategy.
- Sukhumvit corridors: mixed Thai and international range.
- Ari and nearby zones: café and modern Thai clusters.

## Chiang Mai

- Old City: traditional and tourist-accessible mix.
- Nimman: café-heavy and modern restaurant options.
- Night bazaar zones: high-density evening food choices.

## Phuket

- Old Town: heritage and modern fusion mix.
- Bang Tao and surrounding zones: premium dining and resort-linked venues.
- Patong: broad options with quality variance.

## Island and Secondary Areas

- Samui fisherman and beach belts: mixed quality, higher tourist pricing.
- Phangan: split between wellness cafes and nightlife-adjacent spots.
