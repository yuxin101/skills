# Food Practical Guide - Thailand

## Dietary Constraints

- Vegetarian and vegan users should clarify fish sauce and shrimp-paste usage.
- Halal users should focus on verified halal areas and trusted vendors.
- Gluten-sensitive users need simple dish structures and explicit ingredient checks.

## Food Safety Checklist

- Prioritize busy vendors and visible prep quality.
- Favor hot, fresh-cooked dishes over long-exposed items.
- Use conservative choices during first 48 hours in-country if sensitive.

## Alcohol and Night Dining

- Alcohol availability and closing patterns vary by area and period.
- Festival dates and local restrictions can alter service windows.

## Hydration Strategy

- Tropical heat plus spicy food raises hydration demand.
- Use trusted bottled or filtered-water patterns.
