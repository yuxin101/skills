# International and Premium Dining - Thailand

## Where It Is Strongest

- Bangkok: strongest global dining density.
- Phuket: strong luxury-hotel and destination dining clusters.
- Chiang Mai: growing, but narrower at premium tiers.

## Use Cases

- Business dinners and client-facing meals.
- Users with strict dietary constraints needing predictable labeling.
- Celebration and fine-dining itineraries.

## Booking Rules

- Reserve premium tables early for weekends and holiday periods.
- Confirm dress expectations and service charges.
- Match restaurant location to transport reality, especially in Bangkok.

## Budget Ranges

- International casual: THB 300-900 per person.
- Premium tasting and top-tier venues: THB 2,000-8,000+ per person.
