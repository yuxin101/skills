# Salvador and Bahia Coast

## Best For

- Culture-heavy first or second Brazil trip
- Music, history, and Afro-Brazilian context
- Warm-water beach extensions

## Stay Logic

- Barra is the easiest first-time base.
- Rio Vermelho suits food, nightlife, and a more local rhythm.
- Historic-center stays should be chosen for atmosphere, not for friction-free movement.

## Route Design

- 3 nights Salvador plus 3-5 nights on one coast extension is stronger than trying to sample all of Bahia.
- Pick one add-on only: Praia do Forte, Morro de Sao Paulo, Itacare, Trancoso-style coast, or a resort corridor.

## Who It Fits

- Travelers who want culture plus coast
- Couples who want energy without Sao Paulo scale
- Families who can handle some humidity and transfer planning

## Operational Notes

- Holiday timing changes prices and crowd levels sharply.
- Use airport-to-hotel transfers with a clear plan.
- If user wants nightlife and beach in one trip, Salvador often fits better than trying to force Rio to do everything.
