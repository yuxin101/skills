# Manaus and Amazon

## Best For

- Jungle immersion
- River-based logistics and wildlife observation
- Travelers who accept humidity, insects, and slower movement

## Route Design

- Manaus alone is not the same product as Amazon immersion.
- If Amazon is the reason for the trip, bias toward a lodge or structured river plan.
- 4-6 nights is the useful minimum for most users.

## Decision Rule

- Lodge stay: better for wildlife, lower planning noise, more predictable transfers.
- Manaus plus day trips: acceptable for lighter curiosity, weaker for deep Amazon goals.

## Operational Notes

- Water level, weather, and transfer times shape the experience.
- Health prep matters more here than in beach-city routes.
- This block is better paired with one other Brazil block, not three.
