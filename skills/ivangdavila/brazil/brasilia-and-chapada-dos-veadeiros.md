# Brasilia and Chapada dos Veadeiros

## Best For

- Architecture plus interior nature
- Waterfalls, trails, and cleaner two-base planning
- Travelers who want Brazil beyond the coast

## Route Design

- Brasilia can be a short architectural stop or a practical staging base.
- Chapada deserves the real time: usually 3-5 nights.
- This is a good answer for users who want nature without Amazon or Pantanal complexity.

## Drive Logic

- Car planning matters more than urban transport here.
- Rain and road conditions can reshape which waterfall or trail days are realistic.

## Operational Notes

- Better in a dry-season or shoulder-season logic for many users.
- Heat, sun, and trail fatigue should be designed into the plan.
- Pair cleanly with one city only; do not overbuild this into a mega-route.
