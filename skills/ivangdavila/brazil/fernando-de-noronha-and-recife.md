# Fernando de Noronha and Recife

## Best For

- Premium island focus
- Diving, snorkeling, and scenic marine days
- Recife or Olinda city contrast before or after the island

## Noronha Rule

- Noronha should be treated as its own trip block, not as a casual add-on.
- 4 nights is the practical minimum if the island is the point.
- Budget for environmental fees, island transport, and limited inventory.

## Recife and Olinda Logic

- 2-3 nights can work well for city, history, and food before Noronha.
- Recife alone suits users who want Northeast city depth without island pricing.

## Who It Fits

- Travelers who accept higher costs in exchange for marine quality
- Couples prioritizing scenery and water activities
- Repeat Brazil visitors who already did Rio or Salvador

## Operational Notes

- Book early.
- Weather and sea conditions matter to the real island experience.
- If budget or logistics are tight, recommend mainland coast instead of forcing Noronha.
