# Itineraries

## 7 Days - First-Time Brazil

### Conservative
- 4 nights Rio de Janeiro
- 2 nights Foz do Iguacu
- Best for iconic first trip with manageable logistics

### Alternative
- 4 nights Salvador
- 3 nights Morro de Sao Paulo or Praia do Forte area
- Best for culture plus beach without chasing too many flights

## 10 Days - City and Coast

- 4 nights Rio de Janeiro
- 3 nights Salvador
- 3 nights Bahia coast extension

Use when user wants beaches, nightlife, and culture more than wildlife.

## 12-14 Days - Southeast Depth

- 3 nights Sao Paulo
- 4 nights Rio de Janeiro
- 3 nights Paraty or nearby coast
- 3 nights Iguacu or Minas Gerais

Use when user values food, city density, and scenic contrast.

## 14 Days - Nature Priority

- 4 nights Pantanal
- 4 nights Bonito
- 3 nights Sao Paulo or Brasilia as staging city
- 3 nights Rio or Iguacu

Use when wildlife or river activities matter more than beach time.

## 18-21 Days - Advanced Split Trip

- 4 nights Rio de Janeiro
- 4 nights Salvador and coast
- 5 nights Amazon lodge or river-based Amazon plan
- 3 nights staging city
- 4 nights Iguacu or Pantanal-Bonito block

Only recommend this if user accepts multiple flights and some recovery time.

## Planning Rule

Always present:
- A conservative route with less friction
- An ambitious route with clear tradeoffs
- A note on what gets cut first if weather, budget, or fatigue tighten
