# Sao Paulo

## Best For

- Food-focused trips
- Culture, neighborhoods, museums, and nightlife
- Business-plus-leisure extensions
- Weather-resilient city planning

## Stay Logic

- Pinheiros and Jardins are strong all-round choices.
- Itaim Bibi suits polished convenience and business-heavy trips.
- Vila Madalena works for nightlife if user accepts more activity and noise.

## Route Design

- 3-4 nights covers the city well for first-timers.
- Use Sao Paulo as a food and culture anchor, not as a fake beach city.
- If user wants coast, pair it with a dedicated extension rather than squeezing it into every day.

## Strong Pairings

- Sao Paulo + Rio
- Sao Paulo + Minas Gerais
- Sao Paulo + Paraty or Ilhabela-style coast extension

## Operational Notes

- Traffic is the main enemy, not distance on the map.
- Recommend neighborhoods that reduce cross-town travel.
- For users who fear weather ruining the trip, Sao Paulo can outperform beach-first plans.
