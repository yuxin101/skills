# Pantanal and Bonito

## Best For

- Wildlife and slower nature travel
- Clear-water river floating and cave-water activities
- Users who prefer nature over nightlife and city density

## Route Design

- Treat Pantanal and Bonito as complementary but distinct products.
- Pantanal is about fauna and timing.
- Bonito is about water clarity, organized activities, and advance booking.

## Good Timing Rule

- If wildlife is the main goal, Pantanal seasonality matters more than almost anything else.
- If river activities are the main goal, verify weather and clarity conditions before promising the perfect plan.

## Trip Shape

- 5 nights minimum for either Pantanal or Bonito alone.
- 7-8 nights is better if combining both.
- Pair with Sao Paulo, Brasilia, or Rio only if flight logic stays clean.

## Operational Notes

- This is not a casual late-booking segment in peak windows.
- Heat, mosquitoes, transfers, and guide structure should be discussed upfront.
