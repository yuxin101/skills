# Safety and Emergencies

## Safety in Brazil Should Be Operational

- Avoid vague warnings.
- Give context-specific rules for beach days, nightlife, airport arrivals, ATM use, and neighborhood movement.

## Daily Operating Rules

- Carry less on beach days.
- Keep phone use discreet in transit-heavy or uncertain street contexts.
- Prefer app-based or hotel-arranged transport for late arrivals and late returns.
- Use daylight for cash withdrawals and admin errands.

## Emergency Basics

- Keep passport copy, insurer contact, and lodging address offline.
- Know the main emergency numbers before arrival: police, ambulance, and fire response.
- Save one trusted ride-hail app and one offline map before landing.

## When to Escalate Advice

- First-time visitor landing late
- Budget traveler staying far from the plan
- Heavy nightlife plus long commutes
- User carrying expensive work gear or multiple devices

## Tone Rule

Brazil requires smart operating habits, not fear-first trip design.
