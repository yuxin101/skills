# Florianopolis and Santa Catarina

## Best For

- Beach trips with lighter big-city pressure
- Families, surfers, and summer-focused travelers
- Users who want coast quality without defaulting to Rio

## Stay Logic

- North-island areas suit calmer family beach patterns.
- Lagoa and east-side zones fit surf and mixed social energy.
- Car logic depends on season and exact beach strategy.

## Route Design

- 4-6 nights works for a primary beach stay.
- Use this region when the user wants beach quality, food, and a less chaotic urban layer.
- It can pair well with Sao Paulo or a South Brazil road segment.

## Operational Notes

- Summer traffic can dominate the experience if base choice is careless.
- Beach quality varies sharply by wind, surf profile, and family friendliness.
- Great option for users who want Brazil beach life without making Rio the whole trip.
