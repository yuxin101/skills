# Weather and Seasonality

## There Is No Single Best Time for Brazil

| Block | Main seasonality issue |
|-------|------------------------|
| Rio and Southeast | Heat, summer storms, New Year spikes |
| Bahia and Northeast coast | Warm year-round but rain and holiday patterns still matter |
| Iguacu | Heat, humidity, and rain shape comfort more than route feasibility |
| Amazon | Water level and rain change access and experience |
| Pantanal | Wildlife visibility and flood-dry cycle matter a lot |
| Santa Catarina south coast | Summer is strongest but also most crowded |
| Brasilia and Chapada | Dry versus rainy season changes trail and road logic |

## Event and Price Windows

- Carnival can transform prices, crowds, and logistics.
- New Year at beach destinations behaves like a premium-event period.
- Brazilian school holidays can create spikes even when international travelers are not expecting them.

## Planning Rule

- Ask for month first.
- Then match one or two blocks that are strong in that window.
- If the user insists on a weak-season block, show the tradeoff instead of pretending there is no downside.
