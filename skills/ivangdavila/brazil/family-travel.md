# Family Travel

## Family Brazil Is About Friction Control

- Choose fewer bases.
- Prefer calmer beach zones over famous-but-hectic ones.
- Protect against heat, long transfer days, and no-signal moments.

## Best Fits

- Florianopolis and selected Santa Catarina beaches
- Bahia coast with simple transfer logic
- Rio only if neighborhood and pace are carefully chosen
- Recife or Salvador when family wants culture plus manageable beach add-ons

## Less Ideal Family Patterns

- Too many internal flights
- Late-night arrivals followed by immediate transfers
- Amazon or Pantanal plans without genuine enthusiasm for nature logistics
- Historic-center stays with hills or cobblestones if mobility is mixed

## Better Planning Rule

- Price convenience higher than adults traveling solo would.
- Keep one indoor or low-energy fallback for stormy or heat-heavy days.
- Prefer direct transfers and hotel areas with easy food access.
