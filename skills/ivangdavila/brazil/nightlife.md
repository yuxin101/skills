# Nightlife

## Nightlife Needs a Return Plan

- The question is not only where to go, but how the user gets back safely and comfortably afterward.
- Recommend nightlife only when the stay neighborhood and transport plan support it.

## Best-Fit Cities

- Rio for beach-city bars and iconic nights out
- Sao Paulo for depth, variety, and neighborhood choice
- Salvador for music and energy with strong cultural context
- Recife and Florianopolis for more selective scene-based trips

## Planning Rules

- Keep nightlife close to the base when possible.
- Avoid stacking nightlife on top of next-day flights or remote day trips.
- For users with low risk tolerance, downgrade nightlife intensity rather than giving generic warnings.

## Better Output

State:
- Best neighborhood for the scene
- Best return method
- What to avoid carrying
- Whether the user should switch bases instead of commuting across the city at night
