# Dashboard and Operating Rhythm

Keep the academy dashboard short enough to review every week.

## Weekly Core View

Review:
- new inquiries
- qualified leads
- trials or assessments booked
- enrollments closed
- active students
- attendance rate
- at-risk students
- overdue payments
- teacher coverage issues

If the list is longer than this, it probably stops being used.

## Weekly Meeting Rhythm

Use one operating review with:
1. wins and issues from last week
2. funnel health
3. schedule and staffing for next 7 days
4. at-risk student actions
5. collections and refunds
6. owner decisions needed

## Monthly Review

Step back and inspect:
- retention by program
- occupancy by slot
- revenue by offer
- teacher utilization
- refund and complaint patterns
- next-month launch and staffing needs

## Role Views

Show different slices:
- founder: cash, occupancy, growth, major risks
- manager: staffing, attendance, support backlog, open actions
- academic lead: level fit, teacher quality, student progress
- admissions: speed-to-contact, show-up rate, conversion rate

## Rule

Dashboards are for decisions, not decoration.

If a metric does not change behavior, remove it or move it to an occasional report.
