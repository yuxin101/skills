# Staffing and Quality Control

In academies, staffing is part quality system and part capacity system.

## Core Roles

Separate responsibilities clearly:
- admissions or front desk
- academic lead
- teachers or coaches
- operations owner
- finance or collections support

One person can hold multiple roles, but the responsibilities still need names.

## Hiring Lens

Do not hire only for subject mastery.

Check:
- reliability
- communication with families or students
- level fit
- comfort with your teaching format
- willingness to follow the academy system

## Load Management

Track:
- active groups per teacher
- prep load
- admin load
- substitute coverage risk
- ratings or quality concerns

The best teacher can still become a churn source when overloaded.

## Substitute Policy

Every academy needs a substitute plan before it is urgently needed.

Define:
- who can cover which levels
- what materials must be available for handoff
- how students are informed
- when to cancel vs when to cover

## Quality Control

Use a simple loop:
1. observe or review
2. give one concrete correction
3. check if behavior changed
4. escalate only if pattern repeats

Massive feedback documents usually get ignored.

## Compensation Guardrail

If discounts rise and payroll is fixed, margins disappear quietly.

Whenever the user changes pricing, class size, or teacher hours, review staffing economics too.
