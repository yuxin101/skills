# Paper Note: Strategic thinking and contemporary business—Thoughts from an ancient Indian thinker

## Bibliographic details
- Title: Strategic thinking and contemporary business—Thoughts from an ancient Indian thinker
- Author(s): V. K. Gupta
- Year: 2007
- Venue: Paradigm
- Source URL: Google Scholar / journals.sagepub.com listing

## What the paper talks about
The paper connects Chanakya's Arthashastra to contemporary business thought, focusing on strategic thinking, leadership, and the relevance of ancient Indian statecraft to modern management.

## Principles extracted
- strategy should be systematic, not impulsive
- leadership has a moral dimension, not only a tactical one
- governance and disciplined planning matter more than reactive cleverness

## What is useful for Modern Chanakya
This supports framing Modern Chanakya as a strategic system for modern life rather than a collection of quotes.

## Limits / cautions
Ancient statecraft cannot be copied literally into modern corporate or personal life; translation is necessary.
