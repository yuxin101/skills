# Book Note: Corporate Chanakya

## Book
- Title: Corporate Chanakya
- Author: Radhakrishnan Pillai

## What the book is useful for
Useful for seeing how Chanakya is translated into modern corporate and management language.

## Principles likely worth extracting
- role clarity
- leadership and responsibility
- power with discipline
- strategy in organizational life
- practical application over ornamental wisdom

## What to be careful about
Do not copy the book's ideas blindly into the public skill. Cross-check with research and modern ethical constraints.

## Skill modules strengthened
- corporate dharma
- manager architecture
- leadership
- public translation of Chanakya thought
