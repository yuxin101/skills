# Book Note: Never Split the Difference

## Book
- Title: Never Split the Difference
- Author: Chris Voss with Tahl Raz

## What the book is useful for
Useful for practical negotiation language: tactical empathy, calibrated questions, emotional labeling, and keeping difficult conversations productive without surrendering leverage.

## Principles likely worth extracting
- tactical empathy before persuasion
- labels and mirrors as tools for surfacing unstated concerns
- calibrated questions that reopen blocked conversations
- negotiating from preparation, not emotional reactivity
- deal quality matters more than shallow compromise

## What to be careful about
Do not turn negotiation tools into manipulative scripts. The public skill should use these ideas to improve clarity, trust, and assertiveness, not to encourage psychological games.

## Skill modules strengthened
- negotiation and leverage
- conflict communication
- emotional regulation under pressure
- difficult conversations
