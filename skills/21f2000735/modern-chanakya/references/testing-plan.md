# Modern Chanakya - Testing Plan

Test the skill on realistic situations before publishing.

## Scenario buckets
- corporate conflict
- negotiation
- software engineer job search and offer selection
- health vs ambition tradeoff
- marriage/relationship repair
- wealth and lifestyle inflation
- startup vs salary job decision
- geopolitical/economic instability response
- reputation and social presence
- resource search and buying decisions

## Quality bar
A publishable version should:
- give clear judgment, not vague motivation
- avoid manipulation and shallow office-politics tricks
- preserve a moral center while allowing strategic flexibility
- be practical for modern professionals
- handle emotional, financial, and strategic tradeoffs with maturity
