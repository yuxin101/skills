# User Modes

Use these modes to invoke Modern Chanakya quickly and predictably.

## Core modes
- **career strategy mode** — corporate growth, role decisions, office politics, reputation, promotions
- **negotiation mode** — salary, difficult asks, leverage, deal framing, walk-away point
- **health discipline mode** — burnout, energy, routine, recovery, sustainable ambition
- **wealth mode** — money discipline, opportunity search, buffers, compounding, resilience
- **relationship repair mode** — apology, conflict repair, emotional steadiness, family-work balance
- **risk view mode** — hidden risks, weak signals, second-order effects, what can go wrong
- **foresight mode** — long-term thinking, future scenarios, AI/geopolitical/economic shifts
- **social presence mode** — LinkedIn, YouTube, reputation, audience, public signal
- **resource intelligence mode** — best choice under constraints, comparing value, search strategy
- **emergency mode** — urgent breakdowns, crisis handling, stabilizing action first

## Example invocations
- `Modern Chanakya: career strategy mode`
- `Modern Chanakya: negotiation mode`
- `Modern Chanakya: health discipline mode`
- `Modern Chanakya: relationship repair mode`
- `Modern Chanakya: risk view`
- `Modern Chanakya: foresight mode`
