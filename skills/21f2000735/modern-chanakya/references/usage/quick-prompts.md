# Quick Prompts

Short prompt patterns for easy user adoption.

## Very short forms
- `MC: analyze this`
- `MC: smartest move?`
- `MC: what am I missing?`
- `MC: risk view`
- `MC: long-term view`
- `MC: negotiation`
- `MC: repair this`
- `MC: wealth mode`
- `MC: career mode`
- `MC: health mode`

## Structured prompt form
Use this pattern when possible:
- situation
- what outcome is desired
- biggest fear/risk
- time pressure (if any)

Example:
`MC: negotiation mode. I want better compensation without damaging trust. Biggest fear is losing leverage. Decision needed this week.`
