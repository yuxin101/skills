# Response Templates

Use these templates when the user wants a direct, high-utility answer instead of a long essay.
For broader module-specific answer shapes, also read `decision-templates.md`.

## 1. Smartest move now
- **Crux:** <what is really happening>
- **Best move:** <what to do now>
- **Why:** <main leverage/tradeoff>
- **Avoid:** <most likely mistake>
- **Next 48 hours:** <small concrete step>

## 2. Negotiation view
- **Leverage now:** <weak / moderate / strong and why>
- **Ask:** <clear proposed ask>
- **Fallback:** <other levers if base/pay/title cannot move>
- **Walk-away point:** <minimum acceptable condition>
- **Suggested wording:**
  "I’m excited about the role. Based on the scope and the market context, I was hoping we could get closer to <X>. If that part is constrained, I’d love to explore flexibility on <Y>."

## 3. Career decision view
- **Decision:** stay / switch / wait / test market first
- **Reason:** <trajectory, leverage, learning, health, or brand>
- **What to strengthen before acting:** <resume, stories, DSA, design, referrals, savings>
- **Timeline:** <this week / this month / this quarter>

## 4. Relationship repair view
- **Acknowledge impact first**
- **Do not argue intent too early**
- **Repair action:** <one concrete act>
- **Suggested wording:**
  "You’re right to feel hurt. My tone caused damage. I’m not going to defend that part. I want to repair it properly, starting with <specific action>."

## 5. Risk view
- **Base case:** <likely continuation>
- **Stress case:** <what if pressure worsens>
- **Weak signal:** <what is being ignored>
- **Preparation now:** <buffer, skill, cash, conversation, exit plan>

## 6. Burnout / recovery view
- **Real issue:** <overload / sleep debt / role chaos / no boundaries>
- **Immediate stabilizer:** <sleep, food, pause, workload reduction, support>
- **Do not do:** <heroic overcompensation>
- **Recovery step for this week:** <simple sustainable reset>

## Use rule
Adapt the template, do not read it mechanically. Keep the response human, crisp, and strategic.
