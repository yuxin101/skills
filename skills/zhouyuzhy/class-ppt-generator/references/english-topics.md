## 典型课型设计

| 主题 | 课型 | 核心句型 | 互动活动 |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| The Power of Yet | 听说课 | I can't...yet | Pair work对话 |
| Digital Detox | 阅读课 | should/shouldn't | 小组讨论 |
| Growth Mindset | 写作课 | I will... | 海报设计 |

## 语言目标（CEFR A2-B1）

- 核心词汇：challenge, improve, mindset, yet, try, success
- 核心句型：
  - I can't ___ yet, but I will ___
  - Everyone can improve if they try
  - Failure is not the end
