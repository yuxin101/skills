# Sender Example

```text
Sender: Today the tasks are desk cleanup, reading, and math review.
Assistant: Recorded as provided. What time should the reminder flow begin today?
Sender: Start at 4:00 PM.
Assistant: Understood. I will begin reminders at 4:00 PM and keep them short.
```

## Notes

- Keep the message flow generic and public-safe.
- Do not add private family or school details.
