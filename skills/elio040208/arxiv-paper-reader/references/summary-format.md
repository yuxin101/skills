# Summary Template

Use this structure:

## One-line takeaway

State the main contribution in one sentence.

## Problem and motivation

Explain what problem the paper tries to solve and why it matters.

## Method

Describe the key approach, architecture, dataset, or experimental setup.

## Main findings

List the most important empirical or theoretical results.

## Limits and caveats

Call out assumptions, weak spots, missing comparisons, or incomplete evidence.

## Follow-up questions

List three to five concrete questions that deserve deeper review.
