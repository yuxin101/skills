# Voice

**Node type ID:** `14abd0c1-402c-40b8-8ec8-defd951efe01`

Modifies spoken audio using AI -- dubbing into different languages, swapping specific words, and generating lip-synced speech. Valuable for repurposing videos globally, correcting misspoken words, or adjusting terminology without re-recording.

To get the full list of configurable parameters, call `GET /node_type/14abd0c1-402c-40b8-8ec8-defd951efe01`.
