# AI Avatar

**Node type ID:** `b3b4c9e2-2a47-4fa9-8ce8-0c1fa1d7b6ef`

Generates a realistic AI-powered avatar to present or narrate video content. Ideal for creating professional talking-head videos without being on camera. You choose the avatar's appearance, voice, and delivery style, and Mosaic produces a video with natural lip sync and gestures.

To get the full list of configurable parameters, call `GET /node_type/b3b4c9e2-2a47-4fa9-8ce8-0c1fa1d7b6ef`.

[Full docs](https://docs.mosaic.so/tiles/ai-avatar)
