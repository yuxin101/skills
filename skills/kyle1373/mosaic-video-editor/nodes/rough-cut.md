# Rough Cut

**Node type ID:** `e6098dd4-4a6e-470f-981e-2618c11eee21`

Automatically creates a rough cut from raw footage by keeping meaningful sections and removing filler, silence, or irrelevant content based on your creative intent. Useful for quickly condensing long-form footage into a cleaner baseline edit.

To get the full list of configurable parameters, call `GET /node_type/e6098dd4-4a6e-470f-981e-2618c11eee21`.

Place before style-heavy nodes for tighter edits.
