# Silence Removal

**Node type ID:** `a22d0b93-2bbf-479d-8efd-d446163982e8`

Automatically removes awkward pauses, long gaps, and dead air from your video to make pacing tighter and more engaging. Analyzes audio to detect silence and trims it out. Especially useful for podcasts, commentary, educational content, and talking-head videos.

To get the full list of configurable parameters, call `GET /node_type/a22d0b93-2bbf-479d-8efd-d446163982e8`.
