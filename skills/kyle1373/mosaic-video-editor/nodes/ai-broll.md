# AI B-Roll

**Node type ID:** `2160392b-ebe8-4f3d-b81e-fd11bdbbeb77`

Automatically generates or sources relevant B-roll that matches what's being said in your video. Analyzes speech and topics, then generates short video shots for specific keywords, themes, or scenes. Especially useful for talking-head videos, interviews, podcasts, explainers, and educational content that benefit from visual support.

To get the full list of configurable parameters, call `GET /node_type/2160392b-ebe8-4f3d-b81e-fd11bdbbeb77`.
