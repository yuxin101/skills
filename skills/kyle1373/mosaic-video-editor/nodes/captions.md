# Captions

**Node type ID:** `cdccb204-168e-4aec-aa72-480b11e74324`

Add dynamic, stylized captions that match your video's tone and improve accessibility. Ideal for social platforms where sound-off viewing is common (TikTok, Reels, Shorts, LinkedIn). Listens to audio, transcribes speech, and overlays timed captions with your chosen styling.

To get the full list of configurable parameters, call `GET /node_type/cdccb204-168e-4aec-aa72-480b11e74324`.

Key parameters: `caption_style` (colored, scaling, karaoke), `base_color`, `highlight_color`, `stroke_color`, `stroke_width`, `caption_font`, `caption_font_weight` (string, e.g. `"700"`), `caption_vertical_position`, `caption_font_size`, `caption_min_words`, `caption_max_words`.

Place after Reframe if aspect ratio changes are needed.

[Full docs](https://docs.mosaic.so/tiles/captions)
