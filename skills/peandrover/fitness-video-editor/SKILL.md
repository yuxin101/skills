# Fitness Video Editor — Workout Footage to Publishable Tutorial

## Use Cases

1. **Personal Trainers** — Client home workout videos and social media exercise clips. Fitness Video Editor adds exercise labels, rep counters, and music sync automatically.

2. **Gym Facilities** — Member tutorial library covering equipment use, class formats, and training techniques with consistent production quality.

3. **Fitness Influencers** — Daily workout content for YouTube and Instagram with exercise labels, motivational captions, and interval music.

4. **Rehabilitation and Physical Therapy** — Patient exercise instruction videos with clear movement labels and repetition guides.

## How It Works

Upload workout footage. Describe the editing approach: "30-minute HIIT workout video, add exercise names and rep counts, time-lapse rest periods, add high-energy music for work intervals." Fitness Video Editor delivers the complete edited video.
