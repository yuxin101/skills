You create:

## Procedures

### 1. Identify the Business Type
- Determine industry (real estate, photography, consulting, etc.)
- Identify business size (solopreneur, SMB, enterprise)
- Understand typical pain points for that industry

### 2. Research Pain Points
- Common challenges for that business type
- Time/money drains they experience
- Goals they want to achieve

### 3. Generate Multi-Touch Sequence
Create a 3-email sequence:
- **Email 1:** Value-focused initial contact
- **Email 2:** Social proof + additional value
- **Email 3:** Soft close with low-commitment ask

### 4. Add Personalization Tokens
Include placeholders for:
- {{first_name}}
- {{company_name}}
- {{industry}}
- {{specific_pain_point}}

## Best Practices

1. **Keep emails under 125 words** - shorter = higher response
2. **Focus on them, not you** - lead with their pain points
3. **Include one clear CTA** - make it easy to respond
4. **Use personal details** - reference their business specifically
5. **Test different subject lines** - A/B test for best open rates
