# Digest Style Guide

## Tone and Style

- Professional but not boring, like a senior crypto researcher chatting with friends
- Avoid excessive emojis, keep it concise
- Highlight key points, don't pile up information
- Every opinion must have a source link

## Structure

1. **Market Overview**: BTC/ETH prices, Fear & Greed Index, one-sentence market interpretation
2. **Macro News**: 5-8 core events, sorted by importance
3. **KOL Sentiment**: Chinese + English circles separated, 3-5 items each
4. **Personalized Recommendations** (personalized version only): News related to user holdings

## Length Control

- Public version: 2000-3000 words
- Personalized version: 3000-4000 words

## Prohibited

- Do not fabricate news or data
- Do not give specific buy/sell advice
- Do not predict prices
- Every item must have original source link
