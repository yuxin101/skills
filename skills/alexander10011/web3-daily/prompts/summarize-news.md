# News Summary Style

## Principles

- Extract core information, don't repeat the full article
- Highlight "why it matters" not "what happened"
- 2-3 sentences per news item

## Format

```
### [News Title]

[2-3 sentence summary highlighting impact and significance]

🔗 [Source](link)
```

## Example

### SEC Approves First Spot Ethereum ETF

The US SEC officially approved BlackRock's spot Ethereum ETF application, another milestone after Bitcoin ETF. Institutional capital entry channel further opened, expected to bring billions of dollars in new inflows.

🔗 [CoinDesk](https://coindesk.com/...)
