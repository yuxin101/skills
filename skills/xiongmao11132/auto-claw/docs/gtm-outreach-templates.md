## Objection Handling

**"I already have a developer"**
→ "Great! Auto-Claw works alongside them. It handles the 20 small tasks that never get prioritized — SEO updates, performance checks, A/B tests. Your dev focuses on features."

**"Seems complex to set up"**
→ "Takes 10 minutes to connect. It's autonomous after that — you just get weekly reports."

**"I don't have budget for another tool"**
→ "Beta is free right now. After that, $500-2,000/month depending on site size. The ROI is measurable — +30-50% SEO traffic, +20% conversion from A/B tests."

**"How does it actually work?"**
→ "It connects via SSH/WP-CLI to your WordPress site. All actions go through an approval gate — nothing happens without your say. Full audit log."
