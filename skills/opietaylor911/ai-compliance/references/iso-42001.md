# ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Reference
## AI Management System (AIMS) Standard

## Overview
First international standard for AI management systems. Provides a framework for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an AIMS. Certifiable.

## Structure (Clauses)

- 4.5 AI policy (organizational)

## Key Compliance Questions
1. Is there an AI policy in place?
2. Are roles and responsibilities for AI defined?
3. Is there an AI risk assessment process?
4. Is there an AI impact assessment for affected individuals?
5. Is staff training on responsible AI in place?
6. Are AI systems inventoried and documented?
7. Is human oversight defined and implemented?
8. Is there a process for monitoring AI system performance?
9. Are incident and nonconformity processes in place?
10. Is there documented evidence of AIMS operation (audit trail)?
