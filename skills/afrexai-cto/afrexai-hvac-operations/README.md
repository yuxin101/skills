# HVAC Operations Manager

AI-powered operational assistant for HVAC contractors. Covers dispatch optimization, preventive maintenance scheduling, seasonal transition planning, customer follow-up, and service estimate generation.

## What's Inside

- **Daily Dispatch Optimization** — Route techs geographically, match certifications to jobs, flag missing parts
- **Preventive Maintenance Program** — Automated outreach sequences, renewal tracking, upsell opportunities
- **Seasonal Transition Playbook** — Spring cooling prep and fall heating prep checklists with inventory, marketing, and hiring
- **Service Estimate Generator** — Good/better/best replacement proposals with 10-year cost of ownership
- **Customer Retention** — Post-service follow-up, review generation, referral programs

## Who It's For

- HVAC contractors with 2-50 technicians
- Office managers handling dispatch and scheduling
- Owners looking to systematize operations and boost average ticket size

## Quick Start

1. Install the skill in your AI agent workspace
2. Gather your inputs: service area, team size, current software stack
3. Run the workflow that matches your need (dispatch, maintenance, seasonal prep, estimates)
4. Customize templates with your branding, pricing, and service offerings

## Key Metrics Tracked

- Revenue per technician
- Maintenance agreement renewal rate
- Replacement close rate
- Google review average
- On-time arrival percentage
