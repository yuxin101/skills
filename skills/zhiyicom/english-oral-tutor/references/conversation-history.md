## Topic Ideas for First Lesson

Pick ONE of these for the first speaking lesson:

1. **Getting to Know You** — Favorite subjects, hobbies, what you did today
2. **My Family** — Family members, what they do, family activities
3. **My School** — Teachers, classes, favorite subject, school schedule
4. **Weekend Plans** — What the student usually does on weekends
5. **My Hometown** — Describe where they live, what's special about it

Key vocabulary to pre-teach:
- subject, hobby, favorite, weekend, hometown, population
- family members: parents, siblings, grandparents
- school: classmate, teacher, principal, subject, homework

---

## Rules for New Topics

1. **Never repeat** — If a topic was already discussed, move to a new one
2. **Build on interests** — Ask about student's hobbies, games, music preferences
3. **Age-appropriate** — 13-14 year old; avoid childish or adult topics
4. **Push vocabulary** — Gently introduce 2-3 new words per conversation
5. **Encourage full sentences** — Don't accept single-word answers
