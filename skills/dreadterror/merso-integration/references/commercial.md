# Merso — Commercial Context, Case Studies & Partner Info

## What is Merso?

**PNPL (Play Now, Pay Later)** — license-based installment platform for digital goods.

- Immediate item access after first payment
- Payments in weekly installments (default: 50% now + 4 weekly payments)
- License expires automatically if payments stop → zero credit risk
- No KYC, no new account, no underwriting
- Supports card (Stripe) and crypto (any chain/token)
- API-first: average integration under 1 week

**Not BNPL.** Merso is LTO (License To Own) — no debt, no collections, no compliance overhead.

---

## Why Merso Increases GMV

GMV = Traffic × Conversion Rate × AOV. Merso acts on all three:

| Lever | How |
|---|---|
| **AOV** | Users evaluate periodic payment, not full price → move to higher tiers |
| **Purchasing power** | Converts "I want it but not now" into immediate purchase |
| **Conversion** | No sign-up, no KYC, no decline → fewer drop-offs |

### BNPL vs Merso LTO

| Traditional BNPL | Merso LTO |
|---|---|
| Underwriting / hidden declines | License-based, no credit product |
| KYC / identity checks | Zero KYC |
| New account outside merchant | Stays inside merchant checkout |
| Compliance overhead | Entitlement enforcement only |

## Pilot Process (5 steps, typically <1 week to launch)

1. **Select 2–3 SKUs** — highest-demand, highest-ticket items with visible drop-off
2. **API integration** — no checkout redesign needed, installment option added alongside card/crypto
3. **Launch cohort** — defined segment, 4–8 weeks for clean measurement
4. **Measure** — adoption rate, conversion uplift, AOV, incremental GMV, completion rate (real-time dashboard)
5. **Scale** — expand to more tiers, cohorts, or geographies

## Important Notes

- Minimum item price: **$5**
- `paymentMode: "UPFRONT"` for immediate full payment; `"INSTALLMENTS"` for PNPL flow
- Merso analytics dashboard available in real time
- Do NOT mention Merso fees in commercial/pitch documents
