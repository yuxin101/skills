## Validation Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing the AI News Daily Brief.

Every selected story must pass all checks below.

---

## Required Checks for Every Story

1. **Whitelisted source**
   - The primary source is included in `references/sources.md`.

2. **Original source page opened**
   - The specific article or post page was opened, not just a search result snippet or topic page.

3. **Publication time verified**
   - The original publication date/time was explicitly found on the source page.

4. **Within last 24 hours**
   - The verified publication time falls within the last 24 hours for the daily brief.

5. **AI relevance confirmed**
   - The story is directly related to AI, not just general technology or business news.

6. **Not a duplicate**
   - The story is not already represented by another selected item.

7. **Facts supported by primary source**
   - Core claims in the summary are supported by the primary source.

8. **High enough value**
   - The story is important enough to justify inclusion in a curated daily brief.

9. **Best available approved source used**
   - When an approved official source exists for the same event, it should be preferred as the primary source.

---

## Exclusion Rule

If a story fails any one of the checks above, exclude it.

Do not keep a story just to maintain item count.

---

## Final Quality Rule

The daily brief should prefer fewer verified, high-signal stories over more numerous but weakly verified or low-value stories.
