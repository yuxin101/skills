# Sleep Hygiene Guide

## Core Principles

Sleep hygiene refers to daily habits and environmental conditions that support healthy sleep.

## Recommended Practices

- Keep a consistent sleep and wake schedule.
- Limit caffeine late in the day.
- Avoid heavy meals close to bedtime.
- Reduce stimulating screen use before sleep.
- Use the bed primarily for sleep and rest.

## Practical Coaching Notes

Focus on one or two habit changes at a time. Overloading the user with too many changes reduces follow-through.
