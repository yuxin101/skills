# Sleep Environment Optimization

## Key Factors

A supportive sleep environment is usually:
- dark
- quiet
- cool
- comfortable

## Areas to Review

- Light exposure in the evening
- Noise disturbances
- Mattress and pillow comfort
- Bedroom temperature and ventilation
- Clutter or stress-linked environmental cues

## Guidance

Recommend low-cost, practical adjustments first before suggesting major changes.
