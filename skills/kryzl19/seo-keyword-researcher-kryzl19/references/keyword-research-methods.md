# Keyword Research Methods

## Seed Keyword Expansion

Start with 1-3 seed keywords, then expand using:

1. **Google Autocomplete:** Type seed into Google, capture suggestions
2. **Related Searches:** Bottom of Google results page
3. **People Also Ask:** Capture question-format keywords
4. **AnswerThePublic:** Question modifiers (how, why, what, when, where)
5. **Ubersuggest/SEMrush:** For competition and volume data

## Keyword Types by Funnel Stage

| Stage | Intent | Example | Content Type |
|-------|--------|---------|--------------|
| TOFU | Awareness | "what is VPS hosting" | Guide/Explain |
| MOFU | Consideration | "best VPS for AI agents" | Comparison |
| BOFU | Decision | "Hetzner VPS setup guide" | Tutorial |

## Low-Competition Finding
- Target keywords with 3+ words (long-tail)
- Look for "best X for Y" patterns
- Check domain authority of top results — if <40 sites rank, easier to beat
- Newer articles (2024+) = less entrenched competition
- Niche subreddits + communities = underserved topics

## Question Keywords (PAA)
Capture these patterns:
- "how to [task]"
- "what is [concept]"
- "why does [issue]"
- "best way to [goal]"
- "[comparison] vs [alternative]"
- "[number] tips for [outcome]"

These work for featured snippets and voice search.

## Competitor Gap Analysis
1. Find top 3 ranking pages for target keyword
2. Note what they don't cover (gaps = your opportunity)
3. Note what they cover poorly (improve = your opportunity)
4. Check their word count vs. topic depth
5. Identify their backlink sources — earn those links
