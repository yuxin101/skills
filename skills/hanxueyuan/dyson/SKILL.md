# Dyson

Your guide to Dyson — the British technology company known for reinventing everyday appliances.

## Product Lines

### Floor Care
- **V15 Detect** — Laser-equipped cordless vacuum
- **V12 Detect Slim** — Lightweight cordless
- **V8** — Mid-range cordless vacuum
- **Gen5detect** — Most powerful cordless vacuum
- **Ball** — Upright vacuums
- **Submarine** — Wet floor cleaner
- **360 Vis Nav** — Robot vacuum

### Hair Care
- **Supersonic** — Revolutionary hair dryer
- **Airwrap** — Multi-styler (curling, smoothing, drying)
- **Corrale** — Cordless hair straightener
- **Airstrait** — Wet-to-dry straightener

### Air Treatment
- **Purifier Big Quiet** — Large room purifier
- **Purifier Cool / Hot+Cool** — Fan + purifier combo
- **Humidify+Cool** — Purifier + humidifier
- **Zone** — Air-purifying headphones

### Lighting
- **Solarcycle Morph** — Adaptive task lighting
- **Lightcycle** — Intelligent desk/floor lamp

## Technology
- **Cyclone technology** — Patented centrifugal separation
- **Digital motor** — 125,000 RPM Dyson Hyperdymium motor
- **HEPA filtration** — Captures 99.97% of particles
- **Piezo sensor** — Counts and sizes dust particles
