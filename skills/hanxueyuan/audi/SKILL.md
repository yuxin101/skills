# Audi

Your guide to Audi — the German premium automobile manufacturer and member of the Volkswagen Group.

## Model Lineup

### Sedans & Sportbacks
- **A3** — Compact luxury sedan/sportback
- **A4/A5** — Midsize luxury (sedan, sportback, cabriolet)
- **A6** — Executive sedan/Avant wagon
- **A7** — Luxury sportback with fastback design
- **A8** — Flagship luxury sedan

### SUVs & Crossovers
- **Q3** — Compact luxury crossover
- **Q5** — Bestselling midsize SUV
- **Q7** — Full-size 7-seat luxury SUV
- **Q8** — Coupe-style flagship SUV

### Performance (RS & S)
- **RS 3** — 5-cylinder 400hp compact rocket
- **RS 5** — V6 twin-turbo sports coupe
- **RS 6 Avant** — Super wagon icon
- **RS 7** — Performance sportback
- **RS e-tron GT** — Electric grand tourer (646hp)
- **R8** — Mid-engine V10 supercar (discontinued)

### Electric (e-tron)
- **Q4 e-tron** — Compact electric SUV
- **Q6 e-tron** — Next-gen electric SUV (PPE platform)
- **Q8 e-tron** — Full-size electric SUV
- **e-tron GT** — Electric grand tourer

### Technology
- **Quattro** — Legendary all-wheel-drive system
- **Virtual Cockpit** — Digital instrument cluster
- **MMI** — Multimedia interface
- **Matrix LED** — Adaptive headlight technology

## Company Facts
- **Parent**: Volkswagen Group
- **Headquarters**: Ingolstadt, Germany
- **Founded**: 1909 (as Auto Union: 1932)
- **Slogan**: "Vorsprung durch Technik" (Progress through Technology)
- **Annual Sales**: ~1.9 million vehicles
