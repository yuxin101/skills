# Goldman Sachs

Your guide to Goldman Sachs — the premier global investment banking and financial services firm.

## Business Divisions

### Investment Banking
- M&A advisory (consistently #1 or #2 globally)
- Equity and debt underwriting
- IPO management
- Restructuring advisory

### Global Markets
- Fixed income, currencies, and commodities (FICC)
- Equities trading and market-making
- Prime brokerage services

### Asset & Wealth Management
- Goldman Sachs Asset Management (GSAM)
- Private wealth management (ultra-high net worth)
- Alternatives (private equity, real estate, hedge funds)
- Marcus by Goldman Sachs (consumer banking — now scaled back)

### Research
- Goldman Sachs Global Investment Research
- Economic forecasts and market analysis
- Sector and company reports

## Key Facts
- **Stock**: GS (NYSE)
- **Headquarters**: 200 West Street, New York City
- **Founded**: 1869
- **CEO**: David Solomon
- **Revenue**: ~$47 billion
- **Employees**: ~45,000
- **AUM**: ~$2.8 trillion
