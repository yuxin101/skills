# Pfizer

Information guide for Pfizer Inc. — one of the world's largest pharmaceutical companies.

## Drug Portfolio

### Key Products
- **Paxlovid** — COVID-19 oral antiviral treatment
- **Comirnaty** — COVID-19 mRNA vaccine (with BioNTech)
- **Eliquis** — Blood thinner (anticoagulant), top-selling drug
- **Prevnar 20** — Pneumococcal vaccine
- **Ibrance** — Breast cancer treatment
- **Xeljanz** — Rheumatoid arthritis treatment
- **Viagra/Sildenafil** — Erectile dysfunction (now generic)

### Pipeline & R&D
- Oncology pipeline (ADCs, bispecifics)
- Obesity/weight management programs
- Gene therapy research
- Anti-infective programs
- Annual R&D spend: ~$10 billion

## Company Info
- **Stock**: PFE (NYSE)
- **Headquarters**: New York City, USA
- **Founded**: 1849
- **Employees**: ~80,000
- **Revenue**: ~$58 billion (2024)
