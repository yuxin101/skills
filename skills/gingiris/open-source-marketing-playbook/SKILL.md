# Open Source Marketing Playbook

> **This is an alias skill** — pointing to the comprehensive [Gingiris Open Source Playbook](https://github.com/Gingiris/opensource)

## Why Open Source Marketing is Different

Open source marketing isn't traditional marketing. You're not selling a product — you're building a movement. The community IS the product.

### Core Principles
- **Transparency over polish** — Share the journey, not just the destination
- **Community over customers** — Contributors become your best advocates
- **Value over vanity** — Real utility beats star counts
- **Authenticity over advertising** — Developers smell marketing BS instantly

## The OSS Marketing Stack

### Discovery Channels
| Channel | Best For | Effort |
|---------|----------|--------|
| GitHub Trending | Initial spike | High |
| Hacker News | Technical credibility | Medium |
| Reddit (r/programming, etc.) | Community validation | Medium |
| Dev.to / Hashnode | SEO + reach | Low |
| Twitter/X | Real-time buzz | Ongoing |

### Community Building
- Discord/Slack community setup
- Contributor onboarding flow
- Documentation as marketing
- GitHub Discussions strategy

### Content Strategy
- Technical blog posts (SEO)
- Tutorial videos (YouTube)
- Conference talks (credibility)
- Podcast appearances (reach)
