## GTM Strategy Framework

### Phase 1: Market Validation
- Customer discovery interviews
- Competitive landscape mapping
- Value proposition testing
- Pricing validation

### Phase 2: Pre-Launch Preparation
- Content & asset creation
- Community building
- KOL/influencer seeding
- PR & media outreach

### Phase 3: Launch Execution
- Product Hunt launch (24-hour battle plan)
- Reddit & HackerNews strategy
- Social media blitz
- Email campaign activation

### Phase 4: Post-Launch Growth
- User feedback loops
- Iteration cycles
- Channel optimization
- Retention & expansion
