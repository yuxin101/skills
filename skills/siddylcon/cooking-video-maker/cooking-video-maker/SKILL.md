# Cooking Video Maker — Recipe to Publishable Tutorial

## Use Cases

1. **Food Bloggers** — Weekly recipe content needs consistent production: time-lapsed waiting periods, ingredient overlays, technique close-ups. Cooking Video Maker produces a publish-ready YouTube video from raw kitchen footage.

2. **Restaurant Social Media** — Daily specials, seasonal menu launches, and behind-the-kitchen content for Instagram and TikTok. Short-form cooking videos edited to platform-specific durations with auto-captions.

3. **Culinary Schools** — Technique demonstration videos for online course libraries. Step-by-step visual instruction with timestamps, ingredient lists, and exportable recipe cards from the video transcript.

4. **Meal Prep Influencers** — Batch cooking content covering 5-7 recipes in one session. Cooking Video Maker separates the session into individual recipe videos, adds per-recipe ingredient lists, and exports each as a standalone piece of content.

## How It Works

Upload your cooking footage. Describe the output: "5-minute YouTube video for a pasta carbonara recipe, time-lapse the water boiling, add ingredient overlays when each item appears, finish with the plating shot." Cooking Video Maker handles the rest.
