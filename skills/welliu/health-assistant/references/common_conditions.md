# Common Health Conditions Reference

## Cardiovascular

### High Blood Pressure (Hypertension)
- **Normal**: <120/80 mmHg
- **Elevated**: 120-129/<80
- **Stage 1 HTN**: 130-139/80-89
- **Stage 2 HTN**: ≥140/≥90
- **Crisis**: >180/120 (seek immediate care)

### High Blood Sugar (Diabetes)
- **Normal fasting**: <100 mg/dL
- **Prediabetes**: 100-125 mg/dL
- **Diabetes**: ≥126 mg/dL

## Respiratory

### Fever
- **Normal**: 97-99°F (36.1-37.2°C)
- **Low grade**: 99-100.3°F
- **Fever**: >100.4°F (38°C)
- **High fever**: >103°F (39.4°C)

### Oxygen Saturation (SpO2)
- **Normal**: 95-100%
- **Low**: <95% (consult doctor)
- **Critical**: <90% (seek care)
