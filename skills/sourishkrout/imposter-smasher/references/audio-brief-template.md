# Audio Brief Template (3-5 Minutes)

## Narration Structure
1. Opening context (20-30 sec)
2. Meeting objective and stakes (30-45 sec)
3. Participant insights (60-90 sec)
4. Topic intelligence and risks (60-90 sec)
5. Recommended talk track + top questions (40-60 sec)
6. Close with confidence notes (10-20 sec)

## Script Constraints
- 420-700 words total (depends on speaking rate).
- Short sentences and concrete phrasing.
- Avoid jargon unless user domain requires it.
- Tag uncertain claims explicitly: "Low confidence".

## Delivery Voice
- Style: concise executive operator.
- Tempo: moderate.
- Energy: confident, not theatrical.

## Engine Notes
- ElevenLabs: generate MP3, voice preset selected by user preference.
- Chatterbox: generate WAV or MP3 depending available codec.
- Return final path/URL and measured duration.
