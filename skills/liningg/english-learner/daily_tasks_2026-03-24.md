## 📖 下午任务：语法学习（15分钟）

### 语法点：一般现在时（Simple Present Tense）

**定义：** 表示经常性、习惯性的动作或普遍真理

**结构：**
- 肯定句：主语 + 动词原形（第三人称单数加 -s/-es）
- 否定句：主语 + don't/doesn't + 动词原形
- 疑问句：Do/Does + 主语 + 动词原形?

**示例：**
- I like apples.（我喜欢苹果）
- He likes apples.（他喜欢苹果）
- I don't like bananas.（我不喜欢香蕉）
- Does she like oranges?（她喜欢橘子吗？）

**第三人称单数变化规则：**
- 一般情况：加 -s（如：work → works）
- 以 s, x, ch, sh, o 结尾：加 -es（如：watch → watches）
- 以辅音字母 + y 结尾：变 y 为 i 加 -es（如：study → studies）

**练习题（5题）：**

1. I ___（like）reading books.
2. She ___（go）to school every day.
3. They ___（not watch）TV in the morning.
4. ___（do）you play football?
5. He ___（have）a cat.

**答案：**
1. like
2. goes
3. don't watch
4. Do
5. has

## 🎯 晚上任务：综合练习（20-30分钟）

### 第一部分：阅读理解（5分钟）

**短文：**

My name is Tom. I am a student. I am 12 years old. I go to school from Monday to Friday. I have many friends at school. My favorite subject is English. My English teacher is very kind. After school, I play football with my friends. I like sports very much.

**问题：**
1. How old is Tom?
2. What is Tom's favorite subject?
3. What does Tom do after school?

**答案：**
1. Tom is 12 years old.
2. His favorite subject is English.
3. He plays football with his friends.

### 第二部分：听力练习（10分钟）

**对话：**

A: Hello! What's your name?
B: My name is Mary. What's your name?
A: I am John. Nice to meet you.
B: Nice to meet you too. How old are you, John?
A: I am 13. And you?
B: I am 12. Are you a student?
A: Yes, I am. What about you?
B: Me too. What class are you in?
A: I am in Class 7. What about you?
B: I am in Class 6.

**听力任务：**
1. 对话中提到的两个人分别叫什么名字？
2. John 多大？Mary 多大？
3. Mary 在哪个班？

**答案：**
1. John and Mary
2. John is 13, Mary is 12
3. Class 6
