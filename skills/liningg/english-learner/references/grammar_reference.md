# 英语语法参考手册

## 时态

### 现在时态

**一般现在时**
- 用法：表示习惯性动作、普遍真理、固定时间表
- 结构：主语 + 动词原形（第三人称单数加 -s/-es）
- 例句：
  - I study English every day.
  - The sun rises in the east.

**现在进行时**
- 用法：表示正在进行的动作、临时情况、将来安排
- 结构：主语 + am/is/are + doing
- 例句：
  - I am reading a book now.
  - We are meeting tomorrow.

**现在完成时**
- 用法：表示过去发生但与现在有联系的动作、经验
- 结构：主语 + have/has + done
- 例句：
  - I have finished my homework.
  - She has lived here for ten years.

**现在完成进行时**
- 用法：表示从过去开始持续到现在的动作
- 结构：主语 + have/has + been doing
- 例句：
  - I have been working here since 2010.

### 过去时态

**一般过去时**
- 用法：表示过去某个时间发生或结束的动作
- 结构：主语 + 动词过去式
- 例句：
  - I went to Beijing last year.

**过去进行时**
- 用法：表示过去某个时间正在进行的动作
- 结构：主语 + was/were + doing
- 例句：
  - I was watching TV at 8 p.m. yesterday.

**过去完成时**
- 用法：表示在过去某时间之前已完成的动作
- 结构：主语 + had + done
- 例句：
  - I had finished my work before he came.

**过去完成进行时**
- 用法：表示从过去某时间之前开始，持续到该时间的动作
- 结构：主语 + had + been doing
- 例句：
  - I had been waiting for two hours when he arrived.

### 将来时态

**一般将来时**
- 用法：表示将来发生的动作或状态
- 结构：主语 + will + do / be going to + do
- 例句：
  - I will visit my grandparents tomorrow.
  - It is going to rain.

**将来进行时**
- 用法：表示将来某时刻正在进行的动作
- 结构：主语 + will be + doing
- 例句：
  - At this time tomorrow, I will be flying to London.

**将来完成时**
- 用法：表示将来某时间之前将完成的动作
- 结构：主语 + will have + done
- 例句：
  - By next year, I will have graduated.

**将来完成进行时**
- 用法：表示将来某时间之前持续进行的动作
- 结构：主语 + will have been + doing
- 例句：
  - By next month, I will have been working here for five years.

## 语态

### 被动语态
- 结构：be + 过去分词
- 例句：
  - 主动：The teacher teaches the students.
  - 被动：The students are taught by the teacher.

## 从句

### 定语从句
- **限制性定语从句**：用 that、who、which 等引导，无逗号
- **非限制性定语从句**：用 who、which、whom 等引导，有逗号，不能用 that
- 例句：
  - The book that I bought is interesting.
  - My brother, who is a doctor, lives in New York.

### 名词性从句
- **主语从句**：That he will come is certain.
- **宾语从句**：I know that he is honest.
- **表语从句**：The problem is how we can solve it.
- **同位语从句**：The news that he won the game excited everyone.

### 状语从句
- **时间状语从句**：when, while, as, before, after, until, since
- **地点状语从句**：where, wherever
- **原因状语从句**：because, since, as, for
- **条件状语从句**：if, unless, as long as
- **让步状语从句**：although, though, even though
- **方式状语从句**：as if, as though
- **目的状语从句**：so that, in order that
- **结果状语从句**：so...that, such...that

## 非谓语动词

### 不定式
- 形式：to + 动词原形
- 用法：
  - 作主语：To learn English is important.
  - 作宾语：I want to go.
  - 作宾补：I asked him to come.
  - 作定语：I have a lot of work to do.
  - 作状语：He came to help me.

### 动名词
- 形式：动词-ing
- 用法：
  - 作主语：Swimming is good exercise.
  - 作宾语：I enjoy reading.
  - 作表语：My hobby is gardening.

### 分词
- **现在分词**（doing）：表示主动或进行
  - The sleeping baby is cute.
  - I saw him crossing the street.
- **过去分词**（done）：表示被动或完成
  - The broken window needs repair.
  - I heard my name called.

## 虚拟语气

### 条件句
- **第一类**（真实条件）：If it rains, I will stay at home.
- **第二类**（与现在事实相反）：If I were you, I would accept the offer.
- **第三类**（与过去事实相反）：If I had known, I would have helped you.

### 其他虚拟语气用法
- wish 后的从句：I wish I had more money.
- as if/as though 引导的从句：He talks as if he knew everything.
- suggest, order, demand 等词后的从句：I suggest that he go there.

## 介词

### 常见介词用法
- **时间介词**：at, on, in
  - at 6 o'clock, on Monday, in 2025
- **地点介词**：at, on, in
  - at the bus stop, on the table, in the room
- **方向介词**：to, from, into, out of
- **原因介词**：for, because of, due to

### 介词短语
- be interested in, be good at, be afraid of
- depend on, rely on, look forward to

## 连词

### 并列连词
- **表示并列**：and, both...and, not only...but also
- **表示转折**：but, yet, however
- **表示选择**：or, either...or, neither...nor
- **表示因果**：for, so

### 从属连词
- when, while, because, if, although, etc.

## 冠词

### 不定冠词 a/an
- 用法：第一次提到某人或某物时
- 例句：I saw a bird. The bird was blue.

### 定冠词 the
- 用法：
  - 第二次提到的人或物
  - 特指某人或某物
  - 世界上独一无二的事物
  - 最高级前
  - 序数词前
- 例句：The sun is bright.

### 零冠词
- 用法：
  - 专有名词
  - 泛指的复数名词
  - 三餐、球类运动
  - 季节、月份、星期
- 例句：I like cats. Spring is coming.

## 代词

### 人称代词
- I, you, he, she, it, we, they
- me, you, him, her, it, us, them

### 物主代词
- 形容词性：my, your, his, her, its, our, their
- 名词性：mine, yours, his, hers, ours, theirs

### 反身代词
- myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, themselves

### 指示代词
- this, that, these, those

### 不定代词
- some, any, no, all, both, each, every, either, neither
- someone, anyone, no one, something, anything, nothing
- other, another

## 常见错误

### 主谓一致
- 错误：He like apples.
- 正确：He likes apples.

### 时态错误
- 错误：I have seen him yesterday.
- 正确：I saw him yesterday.

### 词性混淆
- 错误：I very like it.
- 正确：I really like it.

### 介词错误
- 错误：I am good in English.
- 正确：I am good at English.

### 冠词错误
- 错误：He is teacher.
- 正确：He is a teacher.
