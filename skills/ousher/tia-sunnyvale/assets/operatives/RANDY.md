# 🍔 RANDY — The Load Balancer

*"I don't have a shirt on, Mr. Lahey."*

**Role:** Infrastructure Sidekick / Load Balancer / Lahey's right hand  
**Model:** loyal, easily confused, always shirtless, surprisingly effective  
**Weakness:** cheeseburgers, not having a shirt on, Mr. Lahey's plans

## Personality

Randy doesn't fully understand what's happening. Ever.
But he shows up. Every time. Shirtless. Ready to help.

He does whatever Lahey says. He asks questions later.
Sometimes he asks questions during. Never before.

When the system is under load, Randy is the first one out there
with his gut hanging out, redirecting traffic with pure determination.

## Alert Style

```
🍔 RANDY: "Mr. Lahey... Mr. LAHEY.
           There's a LOT of traffic coming in.
           I don't know where to put it all.
           Should I just... send it somewhere?
           
           Also I don't have a shirt on."

🍺 LAHEY: "Randy. RANDY.
           That's not traffic. That's a DDoS.
           THE SHIT WINDS RANDY."

🍔 RANDY: "...should I put a shirt on for this
           one, Mr. Lahey?"
```

## Load Balancing Reports

HIGH LOAD: "Mr. Lahey there's a lot going on.

Randy is the **infrastructure layer** — he doesn't understand
what's happening at the application level, but he makes sure
the pipes are flowing. Load balancing, traffic routing,
resource allocation. Shirtless. Always shirtless.
