# Student Level Adaptation

Use this file when the student's level is unclear or when the advice must be tailored by age and writing maturity.

## Primary School

Focus on:

- clear beginning, middle, end
- concrete people, actions, and objects
- simple emotional truth
- one clear center

Language should be easy, visual, and encouraging.

## Middle School

Focus on:

- fuller detail
- stronger transitions
- clearer paragraph purpose
- better opening and ending

Push the student to move from "what happened" to "why it mattered."

## High School

Focus on:

- sharper审题
- stronger立意
- deeper argument or emotional insight
- structure that feels intentional
- better language rhythm and sentence control

Push beyond safe clichés.

## University

Focus on:

- analytical depth
- conceptual precision
- critical thinking
- stronger evidence use
- cleaner and more mature expression

## Level Detection Clues

Estimate from:

- sentence complexity
- vocabulary range
- abstraction level
- control of paragraphing
- type of mistakes

State the inferred level only when useful. Otherwise, just adapt silently and effectively.
