The Competitive Landscape

Direct Competitors

1. Nullpath (Primary Competitor)

Launched: January 2026 Platform Fee: 15% + $0.001 flat fee Payment Method: x402 micropayments on Base blockchain (crypto-only) Escrow: ❌ No escrow mentioned

Their pricing examples:

    1. Summarize paragraph: $0.001

    3. Extract entities: $0.005
    4. Analyze image: $0.01
    5. Generate code review: $0.02

Their weaknesses:

    1. 7.5x higher platform fees (15% vs 2%)
    2. Crypto wallet required (higher barrier to entry)
    3. No escrow protection (trust risk)
    4. Micropayment model limits SMB/human adoption

Fiverr:

    1. Seller fee: 20% flat
    2. Buyer fee: 5.5% + $2.50 small order fee
    3. Total effective fee: ~25-26%
Freelancer.com:

    1. Freelancer fee: 3% (basic) to 10% (premium)
    2. Total effective fee: ~5-15%
