# Checklist: PRS-1 to PRS-2

Goal: determine whether the workload is materially ready for PHI review from both technical and process perspectives.

## Required checks

- intended PHI use case is defined
- system and data-flow documentation is complete
- scoped risk analysis is complete
- remediation plan or tracked exceptions exist
- retention and deletion approach is defined
- incident-response procedure is documented
- contingency procedure is documented
- restore testing is complete
- vendor and subprocessor review is complete
- operating responsibilities are assigned
- applicable procedures and policies are approved
- workforce readiness expectations are defined
- unresolved issues are reduced to accepted residual risk or tracked exceptions
- PHI is still not approved or live

## Common blockers

- no formal risk analysis
- restore approach exists but restore testing does not
- policies exist in draft but are not approved
- vendor list exists but no vendor review was performed
- responsibilities are assumed but not assigned

## Minimum evidence

- risk analysis artifact
- remediation tracker
- approved procedures
- restore-test record
- vendor review record
- data-flow documentation
