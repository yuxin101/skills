# Checklist: PRS-2 to PRS-3

Goal: determine whether the workload has crossed from readiness into explicit authorization for PHI use.

## Required checks

- approval package is complete
- internal approval record exists
- approved workload scope is explicit
- approved environment is explicit
- approved data classes are explicit
- required contracts are complete where applicable
- go-live checklist is signed
- support ownership is assigned
- monitoring and alerting are enabled before cutover
- escalation paths are documented
- required in-scope training for go-live is complete
- PHI is not yet live

## Common blockers

- team says `approved` but no durable record exists
- vendor contract review is incomplete
- go-live checklist is informal or unsigned
- monitoring exists only in theory
- production support ownership is unclear

## Minimum evidence

- approval memo, ticket, or workflow record
- signed checklist
- support roster
- monitoring configuration evidence
- signed BAA or equivalent required contract record where applicable
