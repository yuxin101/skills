# Risk Analysis

Last reviewed: March 17, 2026.

Primary authority pointers:

- HHS risk analysis guidance
- HIPAA Security Rule security management process requirements
- NIST SP 800-66 Rev. 2

## Why this domain matters

Risk analysis is a hard gate for PRS-2 and above. Without a scoped risk analysis, PRS-2 should not be assigned.

## Review questions

- Does a scoped risk analysis exist for this workload and environment?
- Does it identify likely threats, vulnerabilities, and impact?
- Are findings remediated, accepted, or tracked as exceptions?
- Is the analysis refreshed after material change?

## Minimum expectations by stage

- PRS-2: scoped risk analysis completed and remediation tracked
- PRS-3: approval package reflects current residual risk
- PRS-4: periodic refresh and post-change reassessment evidence exists

## Strong evidence

- risk register or assessment report
- remediation tracker
- exception approvals
- reassessment records

## Common blockers

- generic company-wide risk deck only
- no workload-specific analysis
- stale assessment after major architecture change

## Recommended next actions

- complete a workload-specific risk analysis
- track remediation and accepted residual risk explicitly
- define reassessment triggers after material change
