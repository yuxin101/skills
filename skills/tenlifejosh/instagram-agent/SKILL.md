## How the Instagram Algorithm Works (2026)

### Multiple Algorithms
Instagram uses separate AI-driven ranking systems for:
- **Feed**: Posts from followed accounts + recommended content
- **Stories**: Prioritized by relationship strength and posting recency
- **Reels**: Discovery-focused, content-quality driven
- **Explore**: Interest-based discovery for non-followers

### Feed Ranking Signals (by importance)
1. User engagement history with your account
2. Content performance (speed of engagement after posting)
3. Relationship strength (how much the user interacts with your account)
4. Content information (post type, timing, topic, hashtags/keywords)
5. Poster information (account credibility, consistency, niche clarity)

### New Metrics That Matter (2026 Shift)
Likes and comments are no longer primary signals. What now carries significantly more weight:
- **Saves**: Signal long-term value. Content worth returning to.
- **Shares (Sends)**: Signal social relevance. Content worth sending to a friend.
- **Watch time / Completion rate**: For Reels — above 75% = gold
- **Repeat views**: People watching content more than once
- **Comment depth**: Long comments and reply threads (5+ words = "high social relevance")
- **Profile visits from non-followers**: Signal curiosity and trust

### The Originality Score (New in 2026)
Instagram detects recycled clips. Reposting TikTok videos with watermarks or reusing trending content verbatim significantly tanks reach. Always create original content or add a unique spin.

### Topic Clusters
The algorithm tries to understand what your account is about. Accounts that stay within a cohesive niche get placed into "topic clusters" and receive wider distribution within that community. Jumping across unrelated themes confuses the AI and limits distribution.

2. **Carousels**: Encourage swipes = time spent = engagement signal. Great for tutorials and storytelling.

## Reels Strategy (The Growth Engine)

### Length
- 60-90 seconds for storytelling
- 15-30 seconds for viral potential
- Don't artificially cut or pad content

### Hook Structure
- **First 3 seconds**: Must grab attention — text overlay, visual pattern interrupt, or bold statement
- **Last 10 seconds matter MORE than first 3**: Completion rate is king. Build to a payoff.
- **Looping**: End videos with a phrase or visual that mirrors the beginning. Drives rewatch signal.

### Audio Strategy
- 40% trending audio
- 30% original voiceover
- 20% original audio/music
- 10% no audio (text-only)

### Additional Rules
- Always add captions — improves watch time dramatically
- Post 3-5 Reels/week for optimal growth signals
- Trial Reels: new feature shown only to non-followers — use for testing content with no downside risk

### Carousel Strategy
- 5-10 slides is the sweet spot
- First slide = hook/curiosity (treat it like a headline)
- Each slide delivers value AND creates curiosity for the next
- Last slide = CTA (save, share, follow, link in bio)
- Consistent design templates for brand recognition
- Include text overlays — don't rely on caption alone

### Stories Strategy
- Post daily. Accounts with active Stories appear first in search.
- Use interactive stickers (polls, questions, quizzes) — boost engagement signals
- Share new posts as Story reposts for additional visibility
- Behind-the-scenes content performs best
- Use "Add Yours" sticker for viral potential
- Regular Stories = fewer unfollows (confirmed by Mosseri)

## Instagram SEO (Keywords > Hashtags in 2026)

Instagram now functions like a search engine. Keywords matter more than hashtags.

### Where to Put Keywords
- **Username**: Include primary keyword (e.g., "Web Design Expert")
- **Bio**: Natural keyword inclusion for your niche
- **Captions**: Write keyword-rich captions like short blog posts. First 125 characters are most important.
- **Alt text**: Describe images with keywords — helps Instagram categorize content
- **Hashtags**: Still useful but secondary. Use 5-15 relevant, specific hashtags (not generic).

## The 30-Minute Engagement Window Protocol

Instagram tests your post with a small group immediately after posting. If retention and interaction are high, it expands distribution.

**Full Protocol:**
1. Engage with followers for 15 minutes BEFORE posting (triggers relational signals)
2. Post content
3. Reply to every comment within the first 30 minutes
4. Engage with niche content for 15 minutes AFTER posting
5. Share the post to Stories immediately

## Organic Growth System (7 Steps)

1. **Choose a tight niche and stick with it** — topic cluster clarity drives algorithmic distribution
2. **Optimize your profile for search** — keywords in name, bio, and alt text
3. **Post consistently across all formats** — 3 Reels + 2 Carousels + 5 Stories weekly minimum
4. **Engage deeply with your niche community** — like, comment, and share posts from related accounts
5. **Use Trial Reels** — shown only to non-followers. Low risk for testing content.
6. **Collaborate** — use the Collab feature to co-author posts with complementary accounts
7. **Track and iterate weekly** — watch time %, saves per 1K views, shares per 1K views

## Hook Formulas

### Reel Hooks
- **Mistake**: "Stop doing [common thing] — here's what to do instead"
- **Result**: "This one change got me [specific result] in [timeframe]"
- **Secret**: "[N] [topic] secrets that [authority figures] don't want you to know"
- **Story**: "I was [relatable situation] until I discovered [thing]"
- **List**: "[N] [things] you need to [do/stop doing] in [year]"
- **Tutorial**: "Here's exactly how to [achieve desired outcome] step by step"
- **Comparison**: "[Thing A] vs [Thing B] — which is actually better?"

### Pattern Interrupts for Reels
- Text overlay appearing within 0.5 seconds
- Audio switch at the start
- Camera movement (zoom, pan, jump cut)
- "Wait for it" tension building
- Starting mid-action or mid-sentence
- Direct eye contact + bold statement

### Caption Copywriting Rules
- First 125 characters visible before "more" — make them count
- Include keywords naturally (Instagram SEO)
- End with a CTA (save, share, comment, link in bio)
- Use a conversational tone
- Storytelling > bullet points

- 3 Reels
- 2 Carousels

### Sweet Spot
- 4-5 Reels/week

### Best Posting Times (test for your audience)

## How to Post (Publishing Workflow)

Instagram does not support a public API for publishing from third-party tools for personal accounts. For business/creator accounts, the Meta Content Publishing API is available. Use whichever method applies:

### Option 1: Meta Content Publishing API (Business/Creator Accounts)
```bash
# Requires: Meta Business Account, Instagram Professional Account,
# access token with instagram_content_publish scope

# Step 1: Create a media container
POST https://graph.facebook.com/v19.0/{ig-user-id}/media
  ?image_url={url}
  &caption={caption text}
  &access_token={token}
# Returns: creation_id

# Step 2: Publish the container
POST https://graph.facebook.com/v19.0/{ig-user-id}/media_publish
  ?creation_id={id}
  &access_token={token}
```

### Option 2: Scheduling Tools (Buffer, Later, Hootsuite)
If an integration with a scheduling tool is configured, use its API or webhook to schedule posts. Check `skills/instagram/config.json` for configured tool and credentials.

### Option 3: Generate-Only Mode
If no publishing integration is configured, output the formatted content for the human to post manually:
- Output the caption (copy-ready)
- Output the image prompt or describe the visual needed
- Output the hashtag block separately (paste as first comment for cleaner appearance)
- Flag best posting time based on the account's peak engagement window

**Default behavior**: If no API credentials are configured, generate-only mode is assumed. Always ask: "Do you want me to post this, or should I generate the content for you to post?"
