## CRCR Exam Registration

**When JB is ready (target: Week 10-12):**
1. Go to hfma.org/education → CRCR
2. Create account or log in
3. Enroll in CRCR program (~$375 member)
4. Take the course modules (self-paced, ~10-15 hours total)
5. Schedule the 90-minute proctored exam (online or in-person)
6. Pass at 70% → CRCR designation earned

**After passing:**
- CRCR credential valid for 3 years
- 3 years: Recertification via HFMA (continuing education credits)
- HFMA membership auto-renews credential
