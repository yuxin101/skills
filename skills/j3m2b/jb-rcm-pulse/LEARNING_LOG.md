## JB's Questions (Asked During RCM Pulse)

| Date | Question | Unit/Domain | Answer / Status |
|------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| 2026-03-22 | What's the difference between a clean claim and a claim that gets processed without errors? | Unit 4 / Claims | Clean claim = passes all scrubbing rules BEFORE submission. A claim can have errors and still be processed by the payer — it gets kicked back. Clean = pre-scrubbed at the clearinghouse level before it ever reaches the payer. |
| 2026-03-22 | Is there a difference between a denial and a rejection? | Unit 6 / Denials | Yes. Rejection = clearinghouse error (never reached payer) → correct and resubmit. Denial = payer processed and refused → formal appeal process required. Most practices conflate the two. |
| 2026-03-22 | What's the difference between a charge master and a fee schedule? | Unit 3 / Charge Capture | Charge master (CDM) = what the hospital CHARGES (internal). Fee schedule = what the PAYER AGREES TO PAY (contractual). Medicare fee schedule is government-mandated. Commercial fee schedules are negotiated. The CDM rate is often meaningless — payers pay their contracted rates regardless. |
