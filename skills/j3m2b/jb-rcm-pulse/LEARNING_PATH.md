## The Goal
**CRCR certification** (Certified Revenue Cycle Representative) — HFMA's national certification.
20-week structured curriculum. 8 units. Covers front-end → middle → back-end of the revenue cycle.

---

## The 8 HFMA CRCR Units

| Unit | Domain | Weeks | Theme |
|------|--------|-------|-------|
| 1 | Revenue Cycle in Health Care | 1 | Big picture, how the pieces connect |
| 2 | Pre-Service — Financial Care | 2-4 | Scheduling, registration, eligibility, prior auth, financial counseling |
| 3 | Time of Service — Financial Care | 5-6 | POS collections, documentation, charge capture |
| 4 | Claims Submission + Reimbursement | 7-9 | Clean claims, EDI, payer contracts, fee schedules, DRG, value-based |
| 5 | Payment, Adjustments, Collections | 10-11 | ERA/EFT, auto-posting, patient collections, payment plans |
| 6 | AR Follow-Up + Denial Management | 12-14 | AR aging, root cause analysis, appeals, write-offs |
| 7 | Regulatory + Compliance | 15-17 | HIPAA, CMS, Medicare/Medicaid, fraud/abuse, No Surprises Act |
| 8 | Data, Analytics + Performance | 18-20 | KPIs, benchmarking, RCM tech stack, operational excellence |

## How This Works

**Every RCM Pulse (Mon-Fri 8 AM CDT):**
1. Concept of the Day from the current week's CRCR unit
2. Industry news mapped to that week's domain
3. JB checkpoint question drawn from the week's reading
4. JB's answers → logged to LEARNING_LOG.md

**Every Friday:**
- 3-question rapid-fire checkpoint from the week's material
- Claw evaluates and updates the LEARNING_LOG
- Areas of confusion flagged for deeper dive

**Every 3 weeks (end of each unit):**
- Full unit assessment: Can JB explain the domain without looking anything up?
- Weak areas get targeted additional questions
- SparkChange operational angle reinforced at each unit

**At Week 20:**
- JB is ready to sit for the CRCR exam
- SparkChange operational playbook built from the curriculum
- Real client application documented
