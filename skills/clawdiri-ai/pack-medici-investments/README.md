# Medici Investments Pack 📊

**Trade with precision. Size right. Monitor always.**

Two essential tools for active traders: know exactly how much to buy, and always know the market's health at a glance.

---

## What's Inside

### Position Sizer
Calculate risk-based position sizes before every trade. No more guessing.

- **Fixed fractional** — risk a fixed % of portfolio per trade
- **ATR-based sizing** — scale position to volatility (stop = 2×ATR)
- **Kelly Criterion** — optimal sizing based on win rate and R:R ratio
- Sector concentration checks, portfolio-level risk aggregation
- Stop-loss distance calculator

**Example:** *"Risk 1% of a $50K portfolio on NVDA with a $3.50 stop — how many shares?"*

### Market Pulse Monitor
One command, full market health snapshot.

- Trend (SPY/QQQ relative to MAs)
- Volatility regime (VIX level + trend)
- Breadth health score
- Sentiment indicator
- Overall: Green / Yellow / Red signal

---

- Swing traders sizing into breakouts
- Anyone who needs a fast market read before opening positions
