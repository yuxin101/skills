# Business Tools Pack 🚀

**7 automation skills for solo founders building digital products.**

From validating an idea to launching it to collecting social proof — these tools handle the operational grind so you can focus on building.

---

## What's Inside

### Analytics & Tracking Setup
Set up UTM links, conversion pixels, and attribution tracking in minutes. No analyst required.

### AutoSignals — Autonomous Trading Research
An autonomous loop that continuously optimizes trading signal algorithms through agent-driven experimentation. Set it running, come back to improved signals.

### Customer Research & Validation
Pre-launch: scrape Reddit, forums, and competitors to validate your idea before building. Structured output: pain points, willingness to pay signals, positioning gaps.

### Email Automation
Deploy email sequences to ConvertKit or Mailchimp via natural language. Describe your campaign → get a structured sequence with copy and timing.

### Gumroad Product Page Generator
Feed it a JSON spec (product name, features, price, audience) → get a complete Gumroad page with headline, bullets, objection-handlers, and required disclaimers.

### Testimonial Collector
Automated follow-up emails to buyers at day 3, 7, and 14 post-purchase. Personalizes each message. Feeds collected reviews back into your pipeline.

### First Principles Analyzer
Strip every assumption from any topic or decision. Rebuild from what's provably true. Great for strategy calls, positioning, investment theses, or when conventional wisdom feels wrong.
