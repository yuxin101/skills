## 开发信模板

### 冷启动开发信

**主题行公式：**
- `[产品] + [利益点] for [公司名]`
- `Question about [产品] sourcing`
- `[公司名] - [产品] supplier recommendation`

**正文模板：**

```
Subject: [产品] for [公司名] - Factory Direct Supply

Hi [名字],

I noticed that [公司名] sources [产品类别] from China.

We are a specialized manufacturer with [X年] experience in [产品领域].
Our key advantages:
• Quality: [认证/标准]
• Price: Factory direct, [X]% lower than trading companies
• Lead time: [X] days for standard orders

Would you be open to a quick call to discuss your upcoming needs?

Best regards,
[你的名字]
[公司名]
[联系方式]
```

### 价格谈判技巧

**降价策略：**
1. 增加数量 → 降低单价
2. 改变规格 → 降低成本
3. 改变付款方式 → 降低风险溢价
4. 改变交期 → 降低加班成本

**降价话术：**
```
We understand your budget concern. Here are some options:

Option 1: Increase quantity to [X] units → Price: [新价格]
Option 2: Adjust specification to [新规格] → Price: [新价格]
Option 3: Prepay [X]% → Price: [新价格]

Which option works best for you?
```

## 合同要点

### 外贸合同核心条款

1. **产品描述**：名称、规格、数量、质量标准
2. **价格条款**：单价、总价、贸易术语（FOB/CIF/DDP）
3. **付款方式**：T/T、L/C、D/P 等
4. **交货条款**：交期、港口、运输方式
5. **检验条款**：检验标准、检验地点
6. **索赔条款**：索赔条件、索赔期限
7. **仲裁条款**：争议解决方式

### 常用付款方式对比

| 方式 | 风险 | 适用场景 |
|------|------|----------|
| T/T 全款 | 买家风险大 | 小额、老客户 |
| T/T 30%+70% | 风险共担 | 常规订单 |
| L/C at sight | 银行担保 | 大额、新客户 |
| D/P | 卖家风险大 | 信任客户 |

## 物流与报关

### 常用贸易术语

| 术语 | 卖家责任 | 风险转移点 |
|------|----------|------------|
| EXW | 最少 | 工厂交货 |
| FOB | 装船前 | 装运港船上 |
| CIF | 运费+保险 | 装运港船上 |
| DDP | 最多 | 目的地交货 |

### 报关单证清单

- [ ] 商业发票（Commercial Invoice）
- [ ] 装箱单（Packing List）
- [ ] 提单（Bill of Lading）
- [ ] 原产地证（Certificate of Origin）
- [ ] 质检报告（Quality Certificate）
- [ ] 保险单（Insurance Policy，如 CIF）

## 纠纷处理

### 质量问题处理

**客户投诉时：**

```
Dear [名字],

Thank you for bringing this to our attention.

We take quality issues seriously. To help us investigate:

1. Could you send photos/videos showing the problem?
2. How many units are affected? (approximate percentage)
3. Is the issue consistent or random?

We will investigate immediately and provide a solution within [X] hours.

Possible solutions:
• Replacement of defective items
• Credit for future orders
• Partial refund

We value our partnership and will make this right.

Best regards,
[你的名字]
```

### 延期交货处理

```

Please let me know if this works for you.

Best regards,
[你的名字]
```
