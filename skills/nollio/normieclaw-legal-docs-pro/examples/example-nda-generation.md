### RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties wish to explore a potential business partnership combining web development and UX design services (the "Purpose"); and

WHEREAS, in connection with the Purpose, each Party may disclose to the other certain confidential and proprietary information;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the Parties agree as follows:

### 1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means any and all non-public information disclosed by either Party to the other, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by inspection, including but not limited to:

(a) Business plans, strategies, and forecasts;
(b) Client lists, customer data, and vendor relationships;
(c) Financial information, pricing models, and revenue data;
(d) Proprietary project methodologies, workflows, and processes;
(e) Technical data, software, algorithms, and trade secrets;
(f) Marketing plans and competitive analyses;
(g) Any information marked or identified as "Confidential" at the time of disclosure.

> **What this means:** Anything either company shares during your partnership discussions that isn't already public knowledge is protected. This specifically covers your project methodologies, which you asked to include.

### 2. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

The Receiving Party shall:

(a) Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence;
(b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party;
(c) Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose described herein;
(d) Limit internal disclosure to employees and contractors who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations no less restrictive than those contained herein;
(e) Protect Confidential Information using the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care.

> **What this means:** When you receive their information (or they receive yours), you can only use it to evaluate the partnership. You can share it within your team, but only with people who actually need to see it, and they need to keep it confidential too.

### 3. EXCLUSIONS

Confidential Information does not include information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;
(b) Was known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure, as demonstrated by written records;
(c) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of the Confidential Information;
(d) Is received from a third party without restriction and without breach of any obligation of confidentiality.

> **What this means:** If the information is already public, you already knew it, you figured it out on your own, or someone else told you without any confidentiality restrictions — it doesn't count as confidential under this agreement.

### 4. TERM

This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **two (2) years** from the Effective Date. The confidentiality obligations herein shall survive the expiration or termination of this Agreement for a period of two (2) years following such expiration or termination.

> **What this means:** The NDA is active for 2 years. Even after it ends, both sides must keep the information confidential for an additional 2 years. So you're looking at up to 4 years of protection total.

### 5. RETURN OF MATERIALS

Upon termination of this Agreement or upon written request by the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information in its possession, including all copies, notes, and summaries, and shall certify in writing that it has done so.

> **What this means:** When the NDA ends or if either side asks, you give back or delete all the confidential stuff — including any notes or summaries you made from it.

### 6. NO LICENSE OR OBLIGATION

Nothing in this Agreement grants either Party any rights to the other's Confidential Information beyond the limited right to use it for the Purpose. This Agreement does not obligate either Party to enter into any further agreement or business relationship.

> **What this means:** Signing this NDA doesn't mean you're committed to the partnership. It just means you'll keep things confidential while you figure out if you want to work together.

### 7. REMEDIES

Each Party acknowledges that a breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages may be inadequate. Accordingly, either Party may seek injunctive or equitable relief in addition to any other available remedies, without the necessity of proving actual damages or posting a bond.

> **What this means:** If someone breaks this NDA, the other side can go to court and get an emergency order to stop them — they don't have to prove exactly how much money they lost first.

### 8. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **State of Colorado**, without regard to its conflict of laws principles. Any legal action arising under this Agreement shall be brought in the state or federal courts located in Denver, Colorado.

> **What this means:** Colorado law applies, and if there's a legal dispute, it gets handled in Denver courts. Since your business is in Colorado, this is favorable for you.

### 9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, warranties, commitments, offers, and agreements, whether written or oral.

### 10. AMENDMENTS

No modification of this Agreement shall be effective unless made in writing and signed by both Parties.
