# Example: Topic Customization & First-Run Setup

This shows the first-run experience and ongoing topic management.

---

## First-Run Setup

**User:** *(pastes setup prompt, agent runs installation)*

**Agent:**
Supercharged Daily Briefing is installed and ready. Let's get you set up.

What 2-3 topics or industries do you want me to monitor? Just tell me naturally — "AI startups and rare earth minerals" works great.

**User:** I want to track municipal bond distress, AI policy, and venture capital deals

**Agent:**
Great picks. Three topics locked in:
1. Municipal Bond Distress
2. AI Policy
3. Venture Capital Deals

What time should I deliver your briefing each morning?

**User:** 6:30 AM

**Agent:**
Set for 6:30 AM every day. Now let me discover sources for each topic...

🔍 Discovering sources for **Municipal Bond Distress**...
Found 5 sources:
- The Bond Buyer (newsletter) — the industry standard
- MuniNet Guide (blog) — great for regulatory changes
- MuniLand Blog (blog) — independent analysis
- EMMA Filing Monitor (government) — direct MSRB data
- Reuters Municipal (RSS) — wire-speed coverage

🔍 Discovering sources for **AI Policy**...
Found 4 sources:
- The Information (newsletter) — insider scoops
- TechCrunch AI (RSS) — broad tech coverage
- Alignment Forum (blog) — safety and governance deep dives
- EU Policy Monitor (RSS) — European regulatory tracking

🔍 Discovering sources for **Venture Capital Deals**...
Found 4 sources:
- Pitchbook News (newsletter) — deal data and analysis
- Crunchbase Daily (RSS) — funding announcements
- StrictlyVC (newsletter) — curated deal flow
- The Generalist (blog) — deep dives on notable rounds

Want me to keep all 13 sources, or should I drop any?

**User:** Drop The Generalist, I already subscribe to that

**Agent:**
Done — dropped The Generalist. You're running with 12 sources across 3 topics.

You're all set! Your first briefing arrives tomorrow at 6:30 AM. Want a preview right now? Just say "run brief."
