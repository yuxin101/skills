📌 MUNICIPAL BONDS

▸ SEC Proposes Sweeping Muni Disclosure Overhaul
The SEC's 247-page proposal would require real-time material event disclosure from municipal issuers, replacing the current 10-day window. Bond Buyer analysis suggests this could significantly reduce information asymmetry in distressed credits. Market reaction expected to compress spreads on transparent issuers while punishing opaque ones.
Sources: The Bond Buyer, MuniNet Guide
🔗 https://www.bondbuyer.com/sec-disclosure-overhaul-2026

▸ Puerto Rico COFINA Bonds Rally on Revenue Surprise
March sales tax collections came in 12% above forecast, pushing COFINA senior bonds up 3.2 points. Analysts attribute the surprise to tourism recovery and federal infrastructure spending flowing through the island economy.
Sources: MuniLand Blog, Reuters Municipal
🔗 https://www.reuters.com/markets/puerto-rico-cofina-rally

▸ Illinois GO Downgrade Watch Intensified
Moody's placed Illinois general obligation bonds on review for possible downgrade after the state pension funding ratio dropped below 38%. The governor's office pushed back, citing "structural improvements in the pipeline."
Sources: The Bond Buyer, Moody's Research
🔗 https://www.bondbuyer.com/illinois-go-downgrade-watch

📌 AI POLICY & HARDWARE

▸ OpenAI-TSMC Custom Chip Deal Reshapes AI Infrastructure
The $5B partnership will produce custom inference chips by late 2027, reducing OpenAI's dependence on Nvidia. Industry analysts see this as the inflection point where AI companies vertically integrate their hardware stack. Nvidia shares dipped 4% pre-market.
Sources: The Information, Stratechery, SemiAnalysis
🔗 https://www.theinformation.com/openai-tsmc-custom-chips

▸ EU AI Liability Directive Passes Final Vote
Companies deploying AI systems in the EU now face strict product liability under the new directive. Key provisions: burden of proof shifts to deployers for high-risk systems, mandatory incident reporting within 72 hours, fines up to 6% of global revenue.
Sources: EU Policy Monitor, TechCrunch
🔗 https://techcrunch.com/eu-ai-liability-directive-final
