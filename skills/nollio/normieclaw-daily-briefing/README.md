## What Is This?

Stop wasting hours hunting for news, trends, and industry updates. The Supercharged Daily Briefing turns your AI agent into a production-grade research team that:

- **Discovers the best sources** — You tell it your topics, it finds the RSS feeds, newsletters, and expert blogs automatically
- **Monitors them 24/7** — Your agent tracks every source and catches what matters
- **Delivers a crisp briefing every morning** — Right in your chat, before you pour your coffee

This isn't "search Google and summarize." It builds a living source registry, deduplicates stories across outlets, and synthesizes cross-source intelligence into an executive-grade dispatch.

## Quick Start (< 3 minutes)

1. **Download** the skill package
2. **Open** your agent's chat
3. **Paste** the setup prompt from `SETUP-PROMPT.md`
4. **Tell your agent** what topics you care about
5. **Wake up tomorrow** to your first briefing

That's it. No coding. No APIs to configure. No feeds to find manually.
