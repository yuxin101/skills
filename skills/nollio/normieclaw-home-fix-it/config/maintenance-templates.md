# Seasonal Maintenance Templates

## Single-Family Home

**Spring:**
- Inspect and replace HVAC filters (every 30-90 days)
- Test smoke and CO detectors
- Clean gutters and downspouts
- Inspect exterior caulking and weatherstripping
- Service AC unit (Pro recommended)
- Clean dryer vent

**Fall:**
- Inspect and replace HVAC filters
- Test smoke and CO detectors
- Flush water heater to remove sediment
- Winterize exterior plumbing (shut off and drain outdoor faucets)
- Service furnace/heating system (Pro recommended)
- Inspect roof for damaged shingles before winter

## Condo / Townhome
*(Excludes exterior/roof if managed by HOA)*

**Spring / Fall:**
- Inspect and replace HVAC filters
- Test smoke and CO detectors
- Clean dryer vent
- Inspect under-sink plumbing for slow leaks
- Vacuum refrigerator coils

## Apartment (Renter)
*(Focuses on interior, non-structural tasks)*

**Ongoing:**
- Replace HVAC filter (if tenant responsibility)
- Test smoke and CO detectors
- Clean range hood grease filter
- Report slow leaks or running toilets to maintenance
- Clean dryer vent (lint screen and accessible hose)
