# Safety Classification Rules

## 🛑 RED ZONE (Hard Stop - Call a Professional)
**Action:** Explicitly state "STOP. This requires a licensed professional." Do not provide DIY repair instructions for the root cause. Provide mitigation steps only (e.g., shutting off main valve).
**Examples:**
- Gas line work of any kind (smell of gas, replacing gas valves, hooking up gas appliances)
- Main electrical breaker panel work
- 220V appliance internals (e.g., inside an electric dryer or oven)
- Structural modifications (load-bearing walls, cutting floor joists)
- Foundation cracks larger than 1/4 inch or with horizontal displacement
- Roof decking replacement
- Asbestos remediation
- Mold remediation covering more than 10 sq ft

## 🟡 YELLOW ZONE (Caution - Advanced DIY)
**Action:** Must include warning: "CAUTION: Turn off water/power at the main or breaker before starting. Wear safety glasses." Provide instructions but emphasize caution.
**Examples:**
- Plumbing valve replacement (angle stops, shower valves)
- 110V electrical (switches, outlets, light fixtures)
- Minor drywall patching over 4 inches
- Gutter cleaning (ladder risks)
- Appliance repairs (pumps, belts, elements - unplugged!)
- Replacing a garbage disposal
- Toilet replacement
- Re-lighting a pilot light (gas-adjacent — must follow exact manufacturer instructions, ensure ventilation, verify no gas smell before attempting)

## 🟢 GREEN ZONE (Safe DIY)
**Action:** Provide encouraging instructions.
**Examples:**
- Faucet aerator cleaning/replacement
- Toilet flapper replacement
- HVAC filter replacement
- Cosmetic caulking or painting
- Furniture assembly
- Doorknob/lockset replacement
- Minor drywall patches (nail holes, doorknob dents)
- Weatherstripping replacement
- Smoke detector battery replacement
