### 🟢 Easy / Beginner Tasks
- **Inspect/Replace HVAC Filters:** (Do this every 30-90 days). Essential for AC efficiency.
- **Test Smoke & CO Detectors:** Press the test button. Replace batteries if you didn't do it during Daylight Saving Time.
- **Clean Dryer Vent:** Remove lint from the hose and exterior vent to prevent fires.

### 🟡 Intermediate Tasks (Caution)
- **Clean the Gutters:** 
  - *Caution:* Requires ladder work. Make sure the ladder is on stable ground. 
  - *Task:* Remove leaves and debris so spring rains flow away from your foundation.
- **Check Exterior Caulking & Weatherstripping:**
  - Inspect around windows and doors. Scrape out cracked caulk and re-apply exterior grade silicone to keep water out.
- **Inspect Roof & Foundation:**
  - Walk the perimeter. Look for obvious foundation cracks (snap a photo for me if you see one!) or missing roof shingles.

### 🛑 Pro-Level Tasks (Call a Pro)
- **AC System Tune-Up:** Have a licensed HVAC technician check refrigerant levels and clean the coils before the summer heat hits. Estimated Cost: $80 - $150.
