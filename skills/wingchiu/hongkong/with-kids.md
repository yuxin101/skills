# With Kids - HongKong

## Best Bets

| Activity | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Star Ferry | Low effort, high payoff |
| Peak Tram / Peak | Memorable if timed well |
| Disneyland | Easy dedicated day |
| Ocean Park | Good if they want rides plus animals |
| West Kowloon waterfront | Space to run around |
| Ngong Ping cable car | Big visual payoff |

## Family Strategy

- Do fewer neighborhoods per day than adult-only trips
- Plan around heat, bathroom access, and indoor resets
- Sha Tin, Discovery Bay, and larger branded hotels can reduce friction
- Keep Octopus cards loaded to avoid small logistics everywhere

## Food Tips

- Cha chaan teng and mall-based Cantonese restaurants are often easier with kids than intense old-school queues
- Bring cash for small snack stops
