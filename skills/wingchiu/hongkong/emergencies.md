# Emergencies - HongKong

## Emergency Number: 999

Police, ambulance, fire services.

## Safety Reality

Hong Kong is generally easy for travelers in everyday safety terms, but weather and terrain matter more than petty crime for most visitors.

## Main Risks

| Risk | What to do |
|------|------------|
| Heat / humidity | Slow down, hydrate, build indoor breaks |
| Heavy rain / thunderstorm | Move indoor, avoid exposed hikes |
| Typhoon signal days | Recheck all plans and transport |
| Steep streets / stairs | Adjust hotel choice and walking plans |

## Medical

- Pharmacies are easy to find in urban areas
- Private hospitals can be smoother for travelers, but costs matter
- For urgent problems, call 999 or go to the nearest hospital emergency department

## Practical Advice

- Keep hotel address available in phone and on paper
- Watch the Hong Kong Observatory app for weather alerts
- If hiking, do not underestimate how quickly conditions can turn
