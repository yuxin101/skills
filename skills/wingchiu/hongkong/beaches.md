# Beaches - HongKong

## Best Easy Beaches

| Beach | Best for | Note |
|-------|----------|------|
| Repulse Bay | Easiest classic beach | Polished, accessible, not remote |
| Shek O | Casual beach day | Better atmosphere, village feel |
| Big Wave Bay | Surf vibe, easy add-on to Dragon's Back | Smaller beach |
| Pui O | Quieter, Lantau | Good if already on Lantau |
| Cheung Sha | Long stretch, relaxed | Better for a slower day |

## Advice

- Beaches are most useful as half-day add-ons, not always full-day centerpieces
- Summer weekends get crowded fast
- Pair Shek O or Big Wave Bay with an east-side hike
- Pair Pui O or Cheung Sha with a Lantau day
