# Experiences - HongKong

## Worth Doing

| Experience | Why it works |
|------------|--------------|
| Star Ferry | Cheap, iconic, still genuinely good |
| Peak Circle Walk | Best skyline payoff for moderate effort |
| Sheung Wan to Central wandering | Fastest way to feel the city's layers |
| West Kowloon waterfront | Space, skyline, less friction |
| Tram ride on Hong Kong Island | Slow but atmospheric |
| Sai Kung day trip | Water, seafood, reset from towers |
| Night walk in Mong Kok | Density and energy that feels distinctly Hong Kong |

## Museums / Culture

| Place | Note |
|-------|------|
| M+ | Strong choice for modern visual culture |
| Hong Kong Palace Museum | Good if they care about Chinese art history |
| Museum of History | Useful city context when open and current exhibits are good |

## Temples / Heritage

| Place | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Man Mo Temple | Easy add-on on the island |
| Wong Tai Sin | Larger, more active, more local energy |
| Tai Kwun | Better for mixed heritage + dining + urban wandering |

## Good Combinations

- Tai Kwun + Soho dinner
- Star Ferry + Tsim Sha Tsui promenade + West Kowloon
- Sheung Wan walk + tram ride + sunset drinks
