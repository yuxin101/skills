# Telecoms - HongKong

## Best Options

| Option | Best for |
|--------|----------|
| eSIM | Fastest setup for most travelers |
| Airport SIM kiosk | Easy if they want human help |
| Portable Wi-Fi | Groups who want one shared device |

## Advice

- eSIM is usually the cleanest answer for short trips
- Download maps before arrival anyway
- MTR and malls often have Wi-Fi, but don't rely on it as your main connection
