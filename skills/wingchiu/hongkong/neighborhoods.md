# Neighborhoods - HongKong

## Quick Map

| Area | Go there for | Skip if |
|------|---------------|---------|
| Central | Convenience, bars, business | Budget is tight |
| Sheung Wan | Walkable character, coffee, galleries | They need zero hills |
| Wan Chai | Mixed local and expat energy | They want polished only |
| Causeway Bay | Shopping and transit | They hate crowds |
| Tsim Sha Tsui | Harbor access and hotels | They want a local-feeling base |
| Jordan | Food and value | They want luxury atmosphere |
| Mong Kok | Street energy and snacks | Crowds overwhelm them |
| Sham Shui Po | Deep local texture | They only want polished highlights |
| Sai Kung | Seafood and water | Weather is bad |
| Lantau | Big sights, beaches, airport ease | They only have one short city day |

## Best Fits

| Traveler | Area |
|----------|------|
| First-time 2-3 days | Central, Sheung Wan, Jordan, Tsim Sha Tsui |
| Foodie | Jordan, Sham Shui Po, Wan Chai |
| Nightlife | Central, Soho, Wan Chai |
| Family | Sha Tin, Discovery Bay, Tsim Sha Tsui East |
| Outdoors | Sai Kung, Lantau |
