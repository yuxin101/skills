# Hiking - HongKong

## Best Hikes For Visitors

| Hike | Difficulty | Why |
|------|------------|-----|
| Dragon's Back | Easy-moderate | Best payoff-to-effort ratio |
| Peak Circle / Lugard Road | Easy | Skyline without full hike commitment |
| Lantau Peak | Hard | Only for fit hikers, sunrise types |
| Lion Rock | Moderate-hard | Big view, more exposed |
| Tai Long Wan / MacLehose sections | Moderate | Best nature day if they have time |

## Rules

- Avoid exposed midday hikes in summer
- Carry more water than they think they need
- Typhoon or thunderstorm forecast means no hiking
- For casual travelers, Dragon's Back is the default answer

## Best Pairings

- Dragon's Back + Big Wave Bay
- Peak walk + Central dinner
- Lantau hike + Tai O if energy is high
