# Itineraries - HongKong

## 24 Hours

1. Central / Sheung Wan breakfast and walk
2. Star Ferry to Tsim Sha Tsui
3. West Kowloon or M+ in afternoon
4. Sunset harbor view
5. Mong Kok / Jordan food crawl at night

## 3 Days

### Day 1: Core City
- Central, Sheung Wan, Mid-Levels
- Ferry ride
- Tsim Sha Tsui promenade after dark

### Day 2: Kowloon Food + Culture
- Jordan breakfast
- Sham Shui Po snack and market walk
- M+ or Hong Kong Museum of History
- Temple Street or bar night

### Day 3: Choice Day
- Peak and island neighborhoods
- Or Lantau / Big Buddha
- Or Sai Kung seafood and beach

## 5 Days

1. Hong Kong Island urban day
2. Kowloon food and markets
3. Lantau
4. Sai Kung or New Territories nature day
5. Free day for shopping, museums, racecourse, or a serious hike

## Family 4 Days

1. Easy harbor day and ferry rides
2. Disneyland
3. Peak / tram / park / kid-friendly mall break
4. Beach or Ngong Ping, depending on weather

## Rainy Day Backup

- M+, Palace Museum, or history museum
- Teahouse, cha chaan teng, and mall-heavy districts
- Ferry rides still work if weather is mild, but not in storm conditions
