| Area | Best for | Feel |
|------|----------|------|

## Sights That Are Actually Worth It

### Victoria Peak
- Best timing: arrive 60-90 minutes before sunset
- Peak Tram: iconic, but queue can be brutal
- Better strategy when time matters: taxi or bus 15 up, walk the Peak Circle, tram down if line is reasonable

### Central To Mid-Levels
- Escalator route is worth doing once
- Best version: start in Sheung Wan, walk uphill through cafes and older streets, then drift into Soho
- Skip if they hate hills or humidity

### West Kowloon View Alternative
- If they only want skyline photos, Tsim Sha Tsui promenade often gives more payoff for less effort than forcing the Peak

## Fast Half-Day Plan

1. Breakfast in Sheung Wan
2. Walk Man Mo Temple and Hollywood Road
3. Ride Star Ferry from Central
4. Return before sunset for Peak or Kennedy Town waterfront
