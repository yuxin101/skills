# Areas

Ongoing responsibilities. Things you care about that don't have a deadline.

## Structure

Each area folder contains:
- `area.md` — Area charter (scope, principles, success metrics)
- `status.md` — Current status and priorities
- `items.json` — Atomic facts about this area (goals, decisions, metrics)

## Examples

- `people/` — Relationships and mentorship
- `health/` — Physical and mental wellbeing
- `learning/` — Continuous skill development
- `business/` — Revenue and operations

Delete or modify based on your actual areas of responsibility.
