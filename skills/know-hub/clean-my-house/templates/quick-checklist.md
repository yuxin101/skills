# Quick House Cleaning Checklist

## 10 Minutes
- Throw away rubbish
- Gather dishes
- Put obvious clutter into one basket
- Wipe kitchen bench
- Wipe bathroom sink
- Quick sweep of visible floor

## 20 Minutes
- Everything in 10 minutes, plus:
- Clean toilet seat and outside
- Vacuum living room or main walkway
- Make the bed
- Empty one or two bins

## 1 Hour
- Kitchen surfaces and sink
- Bathroom sink, toilet, mirror
- Tidy living room
- Tidy one bedroom
- Vacuum / sweep main areas
- Mop kitchen or bathroom floor

## Deep Clean Focus Areas
- Kitchen grease and appliances
- Shower / toilet / grout
- Dust corners and skirting boards
- Under furniture edges
- Cupboard fronts
- Bedding and laundry reset
