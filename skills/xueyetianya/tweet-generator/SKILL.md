## 专业建议

- Replies and quotes** weigh more than likes for reach
- Images and videos** get 2-3x more engagement than text-only
- Threads** get boosted when each tweet gets engagement
- External links** get suppressed — put links in replies, not the main tweet
- First hour** is critical — engagement velocity determines reach
