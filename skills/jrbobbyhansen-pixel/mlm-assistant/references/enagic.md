## Common Objections (Enagic-Specific)

### "It's too expensive"
- "Over 15 years, that's less than $1/day. How much do you spend on bottled water?"
- "There's financing available. Most people's water savings help offset the payment."

### "I can buy an ionizer cheaper"
- "You can — but look at the plate quality, warranty, and longevity. The cheap ones use 3-5 plates and last 2-3 years. This has 8 plates and lasts 15-20 years."
- "The certification matters too. Enagic is the only ionizer certified as a medical device in Japan."

### "Alkaline water is a scam"
- "I understand the skepticism. Rather than debate the science, would you be willing to try it for a week and see how you feel?"
- "There's research on both sides. What I can tell you is how I personally feel drinking it."

### "I can just add baking soda to water"
- "That makes it alkaline, but it doesn't ionize it or create the ORP that gives it antioxidant properties. It's like comparing a multivitamin to eating real vegetables."

## Demo Script

**The Home Demo (30 minutes):**

1. **Introduction (2 min)**
   - "Thanks for having me. I'm not here to sell you anything — I just want to show you what this water does and let you decide if it's for you."

2. **pH Test (5 min)**
   - Test tap water, bottled water, Kangen water
   - Show the color difference
   - Explain what pH means for the body

3. **ORP Test (3 min)**
   - Show oxidation vs antioxidant properties
   - "Positive ORP oxidizes (ages). Negative ORP is antioxidant."

4. **Oil Emulsification Test (3 min)**
   - Put oil in regular water vs Kangen water
   - Show how Kangen breaks down oil (micro-clustering claim)

5. **Taste Test (2 min)**
   - Let them taste the difference

6. **5 Water Types Demo (5 min)**
   - Show beauty water on skin
   - Show strong acidic sanitizing
   - Show strong Kangen cleaning

7. **Business Opportunity (5 min)**
   - "If you love the water, there's also a way to share it and earn."
   - Brief comp plan overview

8. **Close (5 min)**
   - "What did you like best?"
   - "What questions do you have?"
   - "Would you like to get started as a customer or a distributor?"

## Compliance Notes

**NEVER say:**
- "Kangen water cures [disease]"
- "This is FDA approved" (it's not — it's certified in Japan)
- "Doctors recommend this"
- "You'll definitely make money"

**ALWAYS say:**
- "I personally experienced..."
- "Many customers report..."
- "Results vary / not typical"
- "This is not intended to diagnose or treat any disease"
